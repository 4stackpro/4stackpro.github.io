How to watch Money Heist seasons 1-3 as season 4 is released

Part 4 of the record breaking Spanish thriller Netflix show Money Heist starts today, to the excitement of fans worldwide, after it was viewed on the streaming service a staggering 34 million times.

The show sees eight thieves take hostages and lock themselves in the Royal Mint of Spain, as a criminal mastermind manipulates the police to carry out his plan.

It’s original title La Casa De Papel, was changed to Money Heist for English-speaking audiences before the first season launched, and last August it was named the most-watched Netflix series or film of all time in any language – and the highest non-speaking English show ever.

Netflix previously revealed that 34,355,956 accounts had tuned into the third part of the Spanish-language series within the space of a week.

How can I watch seasons 1-3?

All eight episodes of the fourth part of Money Heist are available to stream on Netflix, so fans can binge-watch the series whenever they want.

Netflix’s Money Heist viewers spot major oversight with Inspector Raquel Murillo’s husband

NETFLIX’S Money Heist viewers have spotted a major oversight with Inspector Raquel Murillo’s husband.

The character - played by Itziar Ituño in the Spanish drama - joined the robbers in season three after initially being put in charge of investigating their crimes.

Her abusive ex-husband Alberto (Miquel Garcia Borda) hasn’t been seen on screen since season two, but after re-watching series three ahead of season four’s premiere, a number of fans have been left confused by his absence.

After Raquel - who goes by the code name Lisbon - agrees to rob the Bank of Spain with Tokyo (Úrsula Corberó), she is caught by the authorities.

The season four trailer sees her being questioned by Alicia Sierra (Najwa Nimri), but viewers think Alberto - who is a forensic examiner - should be brought in to the case too.

Taking to Reddit, one viewer wrote: “I found it really strange that he wasn’t included in the third season, since…you know, his ex-wife is now a criminal that ALSO kidnapped his daughter.

“I really hope he comes back and gets another beat down from our dear Professor.”
