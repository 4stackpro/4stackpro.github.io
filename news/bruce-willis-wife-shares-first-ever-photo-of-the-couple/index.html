Bruce Willis wife shares first ever photo of the couple

Bruce Willis’ wife Emma Heming shares the couple’s first ever photo as he self-isolates with his ex Demi Moore

While Bruce Willis is self-isolating with his ex Demi Moore, his wife Emma Heming has been sharing cute pictures of the couple when they first got together.

Bruce, 65, and Emma, 41, have been together for over a decade now and digging deep into her archives of old photos, the Hollywood actor’s wife managed to find the very first photo they took when they first started dating.

As part of the #firstpicturechallenge doing the rounds on Instagram, Emma posted a shot of her and Bruce cuddling up together by the ocean way back in 2007.

Revealing a cute nickname she has for Bruce, Emma told her followers: ‘Ok! I was nominated so here it is! BeeDub and I Dec. 2007.’

In the picture, Bruce is seen nuzzling into Emma, with his wife joking: ‘He’s clearly SO into me.’

The couple, who share two daughters Mabel, eight, and Evelyn, five, recently celebrate their 11th wedding anniversary together.

Emma joked Bruce is ‘SO clearly’ into her as she shared the throwback snap from 2007 (Picture: Instagram)

Emma, who married Bruce back in 2009, marked the occasion on Instagram with a picture of her and Bruce on a roller-coaster and joked: ‘What a ride kid #11yearanniversary #friendtilltheend #321.’

It’s Bruce’s second marriage as he previously wed ex Demi, 57, who he split from 20 years ago.

The exes share three daughters Rumer, 31, Scout, 28, and Tallulah, 26, and have stayed on good terms since ending their marriage, with Bruce actually spending lockdown with Demi and their family.

Tallulah revealed that Bruce is staying with them when she shared an iconic photo of the Die Hard star and Demi together wearing matching pyjamas for a group shot.

And Bruce’s wife Emma was all for the reunion, as she commented on the pic: ‘Not many can pull that color off! Lookin good squad.’

She even commented on a similar photo shared by Demi and said: ‘At its finest, love and miss you guys.’

Bruce was then seen shaving his daughter Tallulah’s head in another insta upload and quick to respond to that one too, Emma penned: ‘This is actually melting my heart.’

Quién es Emma Heming, la mujer de Bruce Willis que apoya que pase la cuarentena con Demi Moore

Emma Heming es la ausente protagonista del confinamiento de Bruce Willis con Demi Moore y las tres hijas de la expareja. Heming es, en realidad, la esposa de Willis desde hace más de una década pero la cuarentena la está pasando sin su marido en su casa de Nueva York junto a sus dos hijas -también hijas del actor-. Y, desde la distancia, comenta casi cada una de las fotografías que la familia Willis-Moore publica en Instagram. Primero fue la instantánea que compartió Tallulah sobre su “chaotic neutral” familiar. A lo que Hemming respondió: “¡No muchos pueden conseguir ese color! Luciendo buen escuadrón”.

Siguió un día después comentando una foto parecida pero en la que aparecía la familia al completo -parejas y animales incluidos- compartida por Demi Moore y titulada: “Family bonding”. “Os quiero y os echo de menos chicos”, les escribió Heming y añadió unos cuantos emoticonos de corazones verdes.

Y continuó cuando Bruce Willis le rapó el pelo a Tallulah: “Esto me está derritiendo el corazón”. No cabe duda de que Hemming es una más en esta familia bien avenida que -se desconocen las razones- está separada en el confinamiento.

La exmodelo de 41 años y el actor de 65 se casaron en 2009 en la casa de Willis en la isla del caribe Turcas y Caicos en una ceremonia a la que asistió Demi Moore con su entonces marido Ashton Kutcher. Los recién casados se conocieron dos años antes en el gimnasio de su entrenador común recuerda ella misma en su blog en el que incluye recetas, consejos de belleza y selecciona sus prendas de ropa favorita.
