第四位奪冠團員！Harry Styles單曲〈Adore You〉為One Direction創紀錄！

英國人氣歌手、男團One Direction成員之一的Harry Styles去年底推出第二張個人專輯《Fine Line》，第三波主打歌曲〈Adore You〉最近登上Billboard的流行樂播放排行榜（Pop Songs radio airplay chart）第1名，這不僅是他首支奪冠單曲，也讓One Direction成為史上第一個擁有三位現任成員獲此佳績的團體（若加上已離團的Zayn Malik，則有四位）。

▲One Direction團體照，由左至右分別為Louis Tomlinson、Niall Horan、Zayn Malik（已離團）、Liam Payne和Harry Styles。

歌曲〈Adore You〉MV於去年12月6日首播，歷時四個月左右的時間便達到2,600萬次點閱，成績不俗。這首單曲現在更雄踞Billboard的流行樂播放排行榜，經每週統計，4月5日至11日當週已由第3名往前推進到第1名。

Harry Styles此前最好的紀錄是收錄在首張同名專輯的〈Sign of the Times〉，這首歌在2017年拿下排行榜第12名。在單曲〈Adore You〉奪冠之後，包括已離團的成員Zayn Malik在內，Harry Styles成為One Direction第四位登上該排行榜第1名的團員，同時也讓團體創下唯一擁有三位現任成員獲此佳績的紀錄。

其他團員的奪冠紀錄則是Zayn Malik在2016年的〈Pillowtalk〉、Niall Horan在2017年的〈Slow Hands〉，以及Liam Payne在2017年的〈Strip That Down〉。而尚未擁有這項紀錄的最後一位團員Louis Tomlinson，目前最好的成績是他與美國女歌手Bebe Rexha和英國DJ Digital Farm Animals於2017年合作的〈Back to You〉，位列第25。

緊追One Direction紀錄、擁有兩位成員奪冠的團體，目前只有同為美國女團的Fifth Harmony和Destiny’s Child。Fifth Harmony最令人熟悉的成員大概是四度佔據排行榜冠軍的「哈瓦那情人」Camila Cabello；至於Destiny’s Child成員中最出名的，當然就是當今的流行樂天后Beyoncé了，她擁有六次登冠紀錄，且仍持續締造樂壇佳績。

▲Fifth Harmony團體照與Camila Cabello（左一）。

▲Destiny’s Child團體照與Beyoncé（中）。

One Direction members refollow Zayn Malik on Twitter, sparking reunion buzz

The former members of One Direction are giving fans hope in quarantine.

With the Brit boy band’s 10th anniversary coming up July 23, devoted followers have noticed that Harry Styles, Liam Payne, Louis Tomlinson and Niall Horan have all refollowed Zayn Malik on Twitter, although Malik has not followed them back.

Malik, 27, quit One Direction in March 2015, citing “stress” after leaving their world tour. The rest of the members split that August, reassuring fans the break wouldn’t be permanent.

Now, devoted One Direction lovers are interpreting the boys’ refollowing Malik as a sign that the time has come for a homecoming, causing the hashtag #OneDirectionReunion to trend on the social-media platform.

“They all followed Zayn again holy s - -t,” tweets one hopeful.

“Zayn is no longer an ex member on Google [search],” points out another.

Other hints that fans may be right include that the former members have also refollowed the official One Direction Twitter account, all five of the group’s albums are also now back on Apple Music and the group’s former website has been reactivated.

The members themselves have dropped some hints of a reunion as well.

“We’ve got a 10-year anniversary coming up so we’ve all been speaking together a lot over the last few weeks which has been really nice,” Payne, 26, told the Sun on Thursday, “There’s a number of different things that we are all working on to try and make happen and people are forwarding emails around.”

“I think it’ll happen at some point, we’d be stupid not to,” Tomlinson, 28, said on British TV series “The One Show” in January.

In addition to fueling reunion rumors, Harry Styles, 26, has also kept busy during the coronavirus pandemic by supporting the coronavirus fight with a new T-shirt — sales of which are going toward the COVID-19 Solidarity Response Fund for the World Health Organization. The singer revealed in late March that he got stuck in California after his plans to fly back to Europe for the final leg of his “Love on Tour” was canceled due to the novel virus.
