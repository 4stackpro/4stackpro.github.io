森巴精靈鋃鐺入獄，小羅的快樂足球誰人能懂！ - 足球

還記得剛走進足球世界，那時秉哥我只有6歲，對足球還沒什麼觀念，只知道老爸總是會趁著老媽睡著，半夜偷偷跑到樓下看球，而我當時就在想，究竟足球賽有什麼魔力，能讓隔天要上班的老爸再累都要爬起來。我決定也偷偷起來跟著老爸看球，那時電視裡播放的是ENG VS BRA的比賽，整場比賽讓我印象最深刻的就是穿著藍色球衣的暴牙哥。他持球時，老爸總會特別緊張，嘴裡還會唸著：過！過！過，GOAL！

後來慢慢長大才知道，藍色球衣是巴西國家隊當年的配色，那場球賽正是02年世界盃8強賽，英格蘭對壘巴西。而能讓老爸高潮的就是巴西傳奇10號，Ronaldinho。就是他，帶領我走進足球世界，是最初讓我真正體會到快樂足球的森巴球星。

Ronaldinho帶來的不單是足球場上的精彩，也讓我自始至終都謹記，看足球、聊足球、踢足球，無論在哪個階段接觸足球，都別忘記快樂才是初衷。你可以問問身邊愛看球的老球迷，現代歐洲制式化足球大行其道，大家其實都還是懷念著巴西森巴的熱情，嚮往著能再重溫娛樂至上的足球賽事。

（圖/取自秘魯足球網站Depor）

最近打開所有足球報章雜誌，看見Ronaldinho在巴拉圭遭逮捕。他涉嫌偽造假護照，這趟原本要宣傳新書和出席慈善活動的行程，演變成害他鋃鐺入獄的經歷。外界都在歸責，討論誰才是製造假護照的那方，Ronaldinho卻顯得稀鬆平常。被警方逮捕的照片在社交媒體上瘋傳，只見他臉上依然掛著那經典露出暴牙的笑容，毫不在意地出現在媒體面前。

在拘留所裡，Ronaldinho參與了五人制足球，就算獄友規定他只能傳球不准射門，他最終還是贏得了比賽。有趣的是這次他奪得的不是西甲冠軍獎杯，也不是02世界盃獎杯，而是一隻豬。看著他的笑容，讓秉哥我想起許多經典的球賽畫面，也迫不及待打開電腦，想用文字帶讀者們重溫Ronaldinho的快樂足球。

南美森巴血液認證：牛尾巴過人

秉哥我是愛球成痴的巴西迷，平常工作就是寫寫足球，上班前或是下班後都習慣打開Youtube看看球員精華影片。如果你仔細研究各類精華影片就會發現，Ronaldinho的精華畫面很少是進球，而是他充滿節奏感的控球動作。左右晃動讓你無法掌握他下秒會如何處理球，先用右腳假裝要盤帶過人，再將球轉向左邊切入，這經典的搖擺動作被稱為牛尾巴過人。在競技至上的足球世界裡，他證明了充滿花式的森巴球風才是王道。

（圖 / 取自英國媒體The Times）

想起那年Ronaldinho披著巴薩球衣前往斯坦福橋客戰切爾西，面對切爾西鐵衛Terry和Carvalho，還有中場悍將Lampard的夾擊，他選擇站在原地左右扭腰，Carvalho還沒反應過來，Ronaldinho突然就用腳尖將球推向死角，門將Cech來不及撲門，只能眼睜睜看著球入網。這顆進球至今依然被球迷拿出來說嘴，堪稱是牛尾巴過人百科全書的經典之作。

梅羅主宰的現代足球，談論的都是誰拿的金球更多，甚至用成績來評斷球員的表現。但其實很多球迷都已經淡忘Ronaldinho生涯曾經奪得的榮譽和冠軍獎盃，留下的全是經典的足球動作和招式，那些足球回憶裡最難忘的部分。

（圖 / 取自Twitter）

結束這段落前最後再說說我和老爸看的那場02世界杯8強賽，那年貝克漢姆帶領著英格蘭順利復仇阿根廷取得晉級門票，對戰由3R（Rivaldo、Ronaldo、Ronaldinho）組成進攻線的四星巴西，這場8強賽被評為歐洲足球和南美足球的經典交鋒。當Michael Owen率先打破僵局，森巴軍團卻不屈不撓，半場後由Ronaldo扳平比分。比賽膠著，Ronaldinho展現讓人摸不著頭緒的牛尾巴過人，用詭異吊射羞辱了門將David Seaman，最終幫助森巴軍團奠定勝局。

那時還小，還不知道巴西奪得02世界盃冠軍是多有意義的事情，最記得的就只有Ronaldinho那招牛尾巴過人。帶著熱情，小學踢球時總是學著他的控球動作，嚮往著自己也能踢出講求韻律感的森巴球風。學習過人外，小學最愛的還有進球後比手勢，學著Ronaldinho比出66經典手勢。如果問我那時期踢球快樂嗎，我想答案是絕對的，快樂甚至超越了勝負。

娛樂至上：用喝水也能進球

讓我們倒帶回到2013年南美解放者杯，Ronaldinho重返他最深愛的南美洲，加盟巴西足球甲級聯賽的米內羅競技。在對戰聖保羅的比賽中，Ronaldinho看見隊友在右側邊線準備投出界外球，他趁著死球期間悄悄地走到對方禁區內，找對方門將Ceni要水喝，結果把水瓶給回Ceni後，他就藉機轉身沿著底線往右側靠攏。隊友剛好投擲界外球，他接球直接衝向禁區橫傳，助攻隊友完成破門。我想Ceni到現在肯定還在懊悔，早知道當時就不拿水給Ronaldinho喝了，真是好心沒好報。

(圖 / 截取自Youtube）

給大家說說，投擲界外球不存在越位規定，Ronaldinho正是捉緊這點，完美演繹反越位球的特點。他證明了足球世界不單只有勝負，踢得精彩更能贏得球迷掌聲，這也是現代歐洲足球無法看見的表現。參雜著叛逆的森巴球風，加上能帶來掌聲的花式表演，Ronaldinho的快樂足球也許無法成為足球世界之最，但卻能永遠被球迷所傳頌。

跳著森巴舞的南美熱土巴西，曾被視為足球世界最完美的藝術家，那充滿節奏感的森巴球風，更是世界足壇爭相效仿的榜樣。然而隨著南美大陸的足球聯賽日漸衰落，森巴軍團也不再是足球領頭羊，歐洲制式足球取代了熱情奔放的南美足球。讀完這篇文章，但願Ronaldinho能幫助你重新找回最初愛上足球的熱忱。雖然鋃鐺入獄，但Ronaldinho始終都沒有忘記初衷，將足球精神帶到監獄裡延續。我想無論在何時何地，他依然是我們所認識的小羅，是那年快樂足球的創始者。

Party animal Ronaldinho ‘used to take private jet to Cancun after matches and miss training every Monday’ – The Sun

A FORMER team-mate of Ronaldinho has revealed that the Brazil legend would “hop onto his private plane after matches” to party in Cancun.

Ex-Queretaro forward Patricio Rubio, who played alongside the 2002 World Cup winner in Mexico, also admitted that he “never saw him train on Mondays”.

Speaking to Mexican TV channel Azteca about his time playing with one of Brazil’s iconic No10s, he said: “We always played home games on Fridays because each club has their own calendar for the championship in Mexico.

“After the match ended, Ronaldinho would hop onto his private plane and go to Cancun or Playa del Carmen.”

Following their match on Friday, it was normal for players to get the weekend off before returning on Monday.

Well, normal for most players, except for one…

Rubio continued: “He would come back on Tuesday. I never saw him train on Mondays. He was a top player.”

The former Barcelona superstar helped the club advance to their first league final in history - but couldn’t prevent them from losing out to Santos Laguna in the showpiece event.

Ronaldinho’s partying ways are infamous and in 2018 he even had to deny he was marrying two women at the same time.

PRISON STINT

Ronaldinho is currently on house arrest in a hotel room in Paraguay while he and his brother Roberto de Asis Moreira are being investigated for using illegal passports to enter the South American country last month.

They were taken into custody by local authorities before spending over a month in a “Specialised Unit” prison - eventually being granted house arrest last month.

Specialised Unit is a term used in Paraguay which refers to a prison where high-profile prisoners such as politicians and other public figures are kept.

Ronaldinho went to Paraguay earlier this month to participate in a charity event called “Angelical Fraternity”.

His defence claims the passports were given to him as a symbolic gift and he accidentally presented it at the airport thinking it was his Brazilian passport.

The investigation is ongoing.

Ronaldinho appeared to strike unlikely friendships with his fellow inmates, participating in a football tournament and enjoying a barbeque with his new pals.

When he was released after 32 days, some of his fellow inmates even SHED TEARS that they were losing their high-profile new buddy.

The former Barcelona, Paris Saint-Germain and AC Milan star may have a reputation as a solid party boy… but he may not be the biggest of them among his former Brazil team-mates.

Ronaldinho enjoys ‘golden cell’ in locked-down Paraguay capital

Brazilian former soccer star Ronaldinho arrives at a hotel in downtown Asuncion on April 7 to begin his house arrest after his release from jail Brazilian former soccer star Ronaldinho arrives at a hotel in downtown Asuncion on April 7 to begin his house arrest after his release from jail (AFP Photo/Norberto DUARTE)

Asuncion (AFP) - With Paraguay’s capital in lockdown, former Brazil soccer star Ronaldinho is spending his second week under house arrest in an exclusive hotel in Asuncion, where a ballroom has been set up to allow him to keep us his soccer skills, as he awaits trial for entering the country under a false passport.

The 40-year-old World Cup winner and his business-manager brother Roberto are occupying two $350-a-night suites at the otherwise deserted Colonial-style Palmaroga hotel, just a couple of blocks from the government headquarters.

“Yesterday they brought him a regulation football. We set up a room – about 30 meters by 15 - for him to be able to practice his juggling skills,” hotel manager Emilio Yegros told AFP.

“He seems like a good sort. He always has a smile, like his brother,” said Yegros.

“His face has changed from his first day here. When he arrived he was tense and visibly stressed,” the hotel manager said.

The former Barcelona star spends his days working out in the hotel gym, practicing his legendary juggling skills with the ball, and wandering the echoing halls of the 6,000 square-meter (64,000 square-foot) building, refurbished in 2019.

  • Lunch with ‘Ronhi’ -

Ronaldinho and his brother are among 16 suspects in an extensive anti-corruption investigation. Arrested on March 6 for using falsified Paraguayan passports to enter the country from neighboring Brazil, they face up to five years in prison if convicted.

Being held in a police cell for the first 30 days of their detention gave Ronaldinho – his beard grown fuller in that time – and his brother a taste of Paraguayan prison conditions, before a court ordered them released into house arrest at the Palmaroga early this month.

Bail was set at $1.6 million.

The fact that the hotel is owned by the Spain-based Barcelona Group, which has construction projects in Paraguay, is “pure coincidence” and has nothing to do with the star’s former club, said Yegros.

The former player is prevented from receiving visitors in the hotel under Paraguay’s protocols against the coronavirus, which officially has 161 infections to date, with eight deaths.

  • ‘Abusive and illegal’ -

Ronaldinho’s Brazilian lawyer Sergio Queiroz says there are no legal grounds for his detention. “It’s illegal, abusive,” he said. “They did not know that the documents they had were illegal.”

“Leave Ronaldinho alone,” said former Argentine World Cup winner Jorge Valdano, now a coach in Spain.

“The only place Ronaldinho behaved like a troublemaker was on the pitch,” he told a Madrid newspaper.

“One thing is being stupid, as his lawyer called him, and another thing is being a criminal.”

But public prosecutor Alicia Sapriza is sure. “They knew,” she told AFP.

What investigators are trying to determine is if the former star was being used as cover in the plot or whether he willingly lent himself to an alleged money laundering and contraband operation to dispel possible suspicion.

“From the contents of his phone calls that have been examined there was a certain conversation with the brother Roberto in which the documents were discussed,” before they arrived in Paraguay, Saprizia said.

Although prosecutors believe his brother Roberto bears more responsibility in the case, Sapriza says: “Ronaldinho could not be unaware. He cannot claim to have been acting in good faith.”

Ronaldinho and his brother left for Asuncion on March 4 with their own Brazilian passports but showed the Paraguayan passports on arrival.

Mobbed on arrival by 2,000 kids wearing T-shirts bearing his photo, he had traveled to Paraguay to open a casino owned by a Brazilian businessman and his agenda included attending an event organized by a children’s charity.

Prosecutors are expected to limit the charges to using documents with “false content” and the pair will likely be released on bail when court activity resumes following the lockdown, judicial sources told AFP.

A key part of Brazil’s 2002 World Cup winning side, Ronaldinho made club 529 appearances over his 17-year playing career, turning out for top European clubs, including Barcelona, AC Milan and Paris Saint-Germain.
