Alinity banned from Twitch for NSFW wardrobe malfunction

Twitch streamer Alinity controversy was intentional, says Trainwrecks

He believes Alinity is just seeking attention to improve her viewership (Pic: Twitch via Dexerto)

Following Alinity’s Twitch ban for accidentally flashing her breasts, another streamer is convinced she did it intentionally.

Just before the weekend, Twitch streamer Alinity flashed her viewers during a livestream, resulting in her being officially banned for a day, though she chose to extend it to three days.

Like all drama, however, outsiders have felt the need to step in to comment on it, such as another streamer named Trainwrecks, who believes that Alinity’s actions were intentional.

During his own stream, he said that the incident in question ‘doesn’t make sense,’ as reported by Dexerto.

The incident occurred when Alinity tried to put a pillow up her shirt as part of a gag, causing her top to lift too high. But Trainswrecks doesn’t think the nip-slip should have been possible.

‘I’ve never seen someone not only lift the top layer of their shirt, but also lift their sports bra… The pillow’s not going up [there]! It really doesn’t make sense at all, if you think about it.’

He believes that Alinity is simply trying to gain attention to drum up her dwindling views, which he also thinks she’s been doing regularly for a while due to her previous controversies.

‘Every 3-4 months you will see a big event. I think the way she sees it, she will take a small ‘L’ for the greater traction going upwards. Every 3-4 months there’s something. Think about it.’

He also appears to accuse Twitch for double-standards, referencing how another streamer, xQc, was banned for inadvertently showing in-game nudity on his stream, whereas Alinity initially wasn’t banned until after she confirmed she would be taking a three-day break.

my man xqc shows a pixel wearing a bikini & catches a ban, while another streamer who catapulted her career or cat, i forgot which one & pulled her shirt & bra up beavis & butthead style catches 6 sponsors & a new twitch deal — Trainwreck (@Trainwreckstv) April 25, 2020

It should be noted that Alinity is hardly benefitting from these controversies. Last year, she lost sponsorship deals due to animal abuse accusations and, at the time of writing, xQc is one of highest earning streamers on the platform, according to the Gamer Rich List, whereas Alinity isn’t even in the top 100.

Trainwrecks himself has also been caught up in controversy as well. Back in 2017, Polygon reported on his own suspension after he went on a misogynistic rant against women streamers, which he later apologised for.

Twitch, Alinity e il capezzolo: la streamer si auto-sospende dopo le polemiche

Alinity, la celebre streamer di Twitch, ha deciso di auto-sospendersi dopo l’incidente avvenuto nei giorni scorsi, in cui la ragazza ha involontariamente mostrato un capezzolo per un istante.

Non ci sono dubbi che la cosa sia avvenuta per errore, a giudicare anche dal video che trovate in calce, ma non di meno la mancanza di misure da parte della piattaforma ha innescato grandi polemiche da parte della community.

Per provare a chiudere la questione, Alinity ha dunque deciso di auto-sospendersi per tre giorni, a prescindere da cosa farà in concreto Twitch al riguardo.

In una serie di tweet, inoltre, la ragazza ha rivelato di essere stata sospesa in passato dalla piattaforma streaming, ma di non aver pubblicizzato la cosa.
