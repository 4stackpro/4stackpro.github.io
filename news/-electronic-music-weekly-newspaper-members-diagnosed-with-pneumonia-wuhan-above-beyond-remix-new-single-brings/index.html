【電音每週報】團員確診武漢肺炎 Above & Beyond以新混音單曲帶來撫慰

隨著武漢肺炎疫情在全球蔓延肆虐，電音社群也無可避免感受到病毒威力。近期我們聽說了LEMAY與Idris Elba感染的消息，Stomry也由於身體不適正在等待檢驗結果。以New Orleans Bounce風格聞名的DJ Black N Mind則很不幸地因病逝世，得年44歲。

被譽為Trance界最優美夢幻的聲音，來自倫敦的Above & Beyond也未能倖免於疾病的衝擊。就在3月25日，該團傳出團員Paavo Siljamäki確診的消息。在病榻上的Paavo仍不忘鼓勵樂迷與醫療人員攜手合作捍衛健康：「在這個艱困時刻，我非常感謝他們願意冒著生命幫助我。」本週Above & Beyond推出了知名單曲〈Bittersweet and Blue〉的舞池混音版，這首緬懷故人的作品寓意深遠，正可撫慰人們當下悲痛的心情。

雖然大型音樂季因疫情紛紛喊停，但藝人們的發行腳步可沒有停下來。本週Tropical House類型非常活躍，似乎急著搶先一步宣告夏日即將到來。在這些作品中，最值得注意的是Kygo與Zara Larsson合作的〈Like It Is〉。隨著本首單曲發表，Kygo也宣布他的新專輯已經大功告成。樂迷們拭目以待吧。

本週還有許多值得一提的專輯，分佈在各種類型。譬如Psy Trance的《More than Just a Name》、Chill trap的《A Promise Is Made - Remixes》與Downtempo的《Simulcast》等等。在這其中，我們特別推薦《Illusion Of Time》。這張專輯由英國製作人Daniel Avery與Nine Inch Nails鍵盤手Alessandro Cortini攜手合作，迷幻的Drone聲響宛如花瓣在細雨中緩速飄散，最宜春日鑑賞。

Kygo unites with Zara Larsson & Tyga for feel-good tropical tune ‘Like It Is’

Kygo – Like It Is (ft. Zara Larsson, Tyga)

By Lakshay Bhagtani 307

With the release of his latest single “Like It Is” from the upcoming album ‘Golden Hour’ the renowned record producer and DJ Kygo has traveled down the tropical house road once again, much to the delight of his fans. Teaming up with the American rapper Tyga and the renowned Swedish singer Zara Larsson, Kygo has kicked-off the road to his album release in style.

“Like It Is” characteristically defines Kygo’s natural sound that has provided him with all the recognition and admiration over the years. Starting off with some soothing chords and beautifully written vocals, the track eventually makes way for uplifting melodies and feel-good vibes. The element that amazes everyone is the rap sequence by Tyga which is not something you usually get to see in Kygo’s tracks. But it’s quite interesting how he keeps on experimenting with his sounds.

Arriving via Sony Music, Like It Is will likely follow a few more tracks from Golden Hour.

“The good thing about being home all day is that I have a lot of time to work on new music. Social distancing is important, but it doesn’t have to be boring. I hope you all are doing well and staying positive in these tough times.” – Kygo

Kygo taps Zara Larsson and Tyga for first single from forthcoming album

Kygo taps Zara Larsson and Tyga for first single from forthcoming album

Kygo has released new original, “Like It is,” featuring Zara Larsson and Tyga. It’s the first single from the Norwegian producer’s upcoming third album, Golden Hour, out via Sony Music International/Ultra Records/RCA Records.

The intro of the song features enamoring vocals by Larsson, immediately pulling the listener into the catchy commercial fabric of the single. It falls into a radio-friendly tropical house release, leaving the listener believing they have another summer-time Kygo pop single to play by the pool. A surprising set of rap vocals, courtesy of Tyga, jump in after the first drop, giving the release an unexpected twist.

The video features both Larsson and Tyga, who sing with angst throughout an apartment building. The video gives an artistic and colorful visual accompaniment to the single’s compelling audio.
