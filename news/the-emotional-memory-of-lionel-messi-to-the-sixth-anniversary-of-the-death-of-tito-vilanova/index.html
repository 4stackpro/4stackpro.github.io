‘Tito, forever eternal’: Barcelona pay tribute to Vilanova six years on from beloved coach’s death

Barcelona have paid homage to their former head coach Tito Vilanova, six years following his death.

Vilanova died aged 45 on April 25, 2014, after a long battle against parotid gland cancer that cut short the life of a coach in his prime.

The Spaniard’s memory lives on at the club where he played an integral part in the most successful era in its history, both as a manager and as an assistant to his good friend Pep Guardiola.

Barcelona posted this message on the sixth anniversary of the death of Tito Vilanova

Vilanova played an integral part in Barcelona’s greatest era in the club’s long history

Vilanova was assistant to good friend Pep Guardiola and then replaced him as manager

Barcelona posted an emotional picture on their Twitter page with Vilanova on the pitch in front of a banner reading ‘Tito, forever eternal’.

Club legend Andres Iniesta responded to the image with his own tweet which said: ‘Always in our memory’.

Vilanova was assistant to Guardiola, during a golden spell for the club, where they won three LaLiga titles and two Champions League trophies from 2008 to 2012.

While Guardiola was often the public face of the club’s success, Vilanova worked hard behind the scenes, as the club took apart their opposition with a brand of short-passing football that became known as ‘tiki-taka’.

Vilanova would often take charge of preparing the strategy and advise on potential signings, as their dominant era made them to be known as one of the greatest club teams in football history with stars such as Lionel Messi, Xavi and Iniesta among other greats.

Guardiola and Vilanova knew each other back from their days in the youth system at Barcelona.

Andres Iniesta also posted a tribute for his former manager which read ‘always in our memory’

Vilanova was let go in 1990 and played for several lower league teams before getting his chance in the Spanish top flight with Celta Vigo.

It was as a coach though that he gained recognition, and he was the natural fit to take over as manager when Guardiola decided to step down.

Barcelona went on to make a record start to a LaLiga season with 18 wins from 19 games but in December 2012, Vilanova was diagnosed with cancer for a second time.

Lionel Messi was the star player during Barcelona’s golden era with Vilanova and Guardiola

This resulted in assistant Jordi Roura taking charge until the end of March when Vilanova returned to the bench to get Barcelona over the line to win the title.

At the end of the season, Vilanova announced he was resigning so that he could focus on his treatment.

But the way Vilanova attempted to carry on despite his illness drew much admiration from people in the football world and his death in April 2014 drew a considerable outpouring of sadness at his passing.

Karier Emas Tito Vilanova di Barcelona Terhambat Kanker

Jakarta, CNN Indonesia –punya kesempatan emas menjadi pelatih hebat di. Sayang beribu sayang, kanker menghentikan langkahnya.Masa depan Vilanova terlihat begitu cerah ketika ditunjuk sebagai suksesor Pep Guardiola. Blaugrana malah makin solid dan tampil cantik di bawah arahannya.Tak sulit bagi Vilanova untuk mewarisi peninggalan Guardiola. Keduanya sudah bekerja sama menangani Barca B sejak 2007/2008.Vilanova diboyong ke dalam staf pelatih utama Barcelona ketika Guardiola ditunjuk sebagai pengganti Frank Rijkaard pada 2008.Guardiola tak mau lepas dari orang kepercayaannya untuk menempuh tantangan berat di kasta tertinggi. Apalagi, ia menggantikan pelatih dengan nama besar, Frank Rijkaard.Siapa nyana, duet pelatih muda ini justru moncer. Sebanyak 14 gelar -termasuk dua trofi Liga Champions- berhasil mereka raih selama empat tahun bersama.Tantangan lebih dahsyat menanti Vilanova di tahun 2012. Ia harus memimpin Azulgrana sendirian setelah Guardiola memutuskan hengkang pada 2012.Semula, keputusan Barcelona dianggap blunder. Vilanova dipandang sebelah mata dan bukan sosok yang pantas menjadi pemimpin klub raksasa dari Catalunya.Tapi, pria kelahiran Bellcaire d’Emporda menjawab keraguan publik dengan prestasi. Vilanova sukses mengantarkan Barca menjadi juara La Liga pada musim 2012/2013 dengan raihan total 100 poin.Sederet rekor individu pun mengekor. Satu di antaranya membawa Barcelona mencetak rekor baru dengan mengoleksi 115 gol dalam 38 partai di liga.Sayang, kesehatan Vilanova menurun drastis pada Juli 2013 dan memutuskan mundur dari jabatannya. Ia harus berjuang melawan kanker tenggorokan yang telah menggerogoti sejak 2011.Pada 25 April 2014 Barcelona berkabung. Vilanova tak sanggup lagi berjuang melawan kanker dan terpaksa meninggalkan istri dan dua anak tercinta. Tapi, namanya selalu abadi di hati pendukung Barcelona.Tito, Per Sempre Etern.
