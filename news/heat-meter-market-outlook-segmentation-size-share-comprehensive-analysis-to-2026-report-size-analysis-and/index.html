In Depth Analysis of the Heat Meter Market A heat meter is an instrument for measuring the heat energy generated by a source. For measuring changes in temperature between its outflow and returns leg and the rate of flow of heat transfer fluid, a device named heat meter is useful. There are three components of a heat meter, namely, a calculator, a flow sensor, and a pair of temperature sensors. For reducing the effect of climate change due to human activities, governments are giving incentives to industries and citizens for installing heat meters to limit and monitor their thermal energy use. Three kinds of technologies are used in heat meters, such as mechanical, ultrasonic, and electromagnetic technology. Ultrasonic heat meters are expected to dominate the market as they don't have moving parts resulting in more accurate reading.
