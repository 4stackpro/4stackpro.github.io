MAKAUT starts online classes as varsity shuts amid Corona scare

Kolkata: The Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT) has started holding online classes for its students in the wake of the closure of university till April 15, as a precautionary measure to prevent spreading of COVID-19.

“We are using Artificial Intelligence (AI) platform, Learning Management System and video conference technique for the virtual classes. Apart from the classes, the performance assessment is also being done in online mode with the AI-based platform that we have developed,” said Saikat Maitra, Vice-Chancellor of MAKAUT.

The cloud-based system enables the teacher to take classes from their respective residences. Students have already been handed over their respective routine for the virtual classroom. On average a student is attending three classes of 45 minutes duration each.

The virtual classroom system at MAKAUT has been existing for some time and the basic infrastructure was already there.

“We have strengthened the infrastructure and we are hopeful that there will hardly be any inconvenience to the students with this forced lockdown at home,” a senior official of the university said.

The university for the last few months was making preparations to go for virtual classroom facilities in case of any emergent situation so there was not much difficulty in the switch over process.

The university has several online courses for which the basic infrastructure for online mode study was already in place.

“Our internal tests are already conducted online and we will soon develop facilities so that semester examinations can be held online,” said Maitra.

The university authorities are sending the online link of the virtual classes to the students who fail to attend the online classes due to internet problem.

The university has already provided training to teachers and students so that they can easily cope up with the AI-based virtual classroom concept.

“There is a login system for attending the virtual classes and the attendance of students will be evaluated based on

this login mode,” the official added.

MAKAUT Prepares Hand Sanitizer for Free Distribution and Campaigns Against Corona Virus

MAKAUT Prepares Hand Sanitizer for Free Distribution and Campaigns Against Corona Virus

Kolkata, March 21, 2020: MAKAUT Prepares Hand Sanitizer for Free Distribution and Campaigns Against Corona Virus. The Fight Against Corona Virus: A Great Leap Forward by MAKAUT, WB.

Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), WB is taking an active part in the fight against Corona Virus, under the skilful guidance of its Vice Chancellor Prof. (Dr.) Saikat Maitra. He has prepared hand sanitizer according to the guidelines provided by World Health Organization (WHO) in his own initiative. It is being produced at the Haringhata and Salt Lake Campuses on war footing. Initially it has been distributed amongst the staff of MAKAUT. Prof. Maitra said, “MAKAUT will soon produce it in a large scale, so that the needy common people and various Government Departments can get it free of cost.”

Distribution of Hand Sanitizers prepared by MAKAUT will start soon as a part of the University’s commitment towards social responsibility. The University has also distributed masks to its staff and is campaigning against the spread of Corona Virus.

MAKAUT has also taken an initiative to meet the demand-supply gap of sanitizer in the market. The Assistant Registrar of MAKAUT Mr. Anup Kumar Mukherjee said, “Everyday 1000 bottles of sanitizer are being prepared. Each bottle is of 100ml. These are being sent to the College of Medicine and JNM Hospital, Kalyani, Haringhata Municipality and BDO Office.”

The faculties like Dr. Somdatta Chakravortty, Dr. Madhumita Das Sarkar, Dr. Prasanta Narayan Dutta and several staffs took part in the preparing and bottling of hand sanitizers.

The Vice-Chancellor of MAKAUT always tries to inculcate social consciousness within the MAKAUT family and amongst the students. MAKAUT is making this fight against Corona in a war footing and is developing a team spirit and has the will of iron for protecting public health and serve the needy common people.

MAKAUT Begins Manufacturing Hand Sanitizers For Free Distribution

A group of students, teachers, and officials of Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT) began manufacturing sanitizers at its laboratory for free distribution. The University authority took the initiative since the hand sanitizers went out of the market due to huge demand in the wake of the Covid 19 threat. Advised by the University vice-chancellor Professor Saikat Maitra the chemistry department took the initiative and till Thursday evening the team has been able to manufacture 850 units of 100 ml in size. University’s Assistant Registrar Anup Mukherjee said, “There is a crisis of sanitizer in the market. People have been madly looking for it. So we took the initiative to manufacture the sanitizer for free distribution among people and hospitals following the WHO guidelines”. Only a handful of students from outsider state who could not leave the campus joined with teachers and officials to materialize the endeavor, he added. Boiled water, ethyl alcohol, glycerol, hydrogen peroxide have been used to manufacture the sanitizer. “We have planned to manufacture daily 1000 units each from its Haringhata and Salt lake campus daily from Friday until things get normalized”, said Assistant Registrar Professor Mukherjee.
