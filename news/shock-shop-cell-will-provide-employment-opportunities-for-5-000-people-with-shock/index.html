Şok Marketler, Cepte Şok ile 5.000 kişiye istihdam imkanı sağlayacak

Geçtiğimiz hafta Şok Marketler’in yenilenen online market servisi uygulaması Cepte Şok’un detaylarını sizlere aktarmıştık. Bizim özel haber olarak yayınladığımız Cepte Şok’u, bugün resmi olarak duyurdu.

Cepte Şok ile ücretsiz adrese teslimat hizmetini başlattığını duyuran indirim marketleri zinciri, resmi açıklamasıyla istihdam konusundaki ön görülerimizi de doğruladı. Şok Marketler, Cepte Şok’un da katkısıyla 2020’de 5.000 kişiyi istihdam etmeyi hedefliyor.

Kullanıcıların sipariş teslimatları aynı gün içinde ek bir ücret ödemeden gerçekleşeceğini hatırlatalım. Bunun yanı sıra Cepte Şok’tan sipariş verilen ürünlerin fiyatlarının Şok Market fiyatlarıyla aynı olacağını tekrarlamakta da fayda var. Şok Marketler CEO’su Uğur Demirel, Cepte Şok uygulamasıyla ilgili yaptığı açıklamada COVİD-19 salgınında topluma destek olmak için attıkları adımlara da yer verdi:

Şok Marketler started free address delivery service

Developing the shopping experience with innovative and digital solutions in line with the rapidly changing expectations and needs of the customers, Şok Marketler renewed the application of Cepte Şok. Users’ orders are now delivered free of charge to their door on the same day with online payment via the Mobile Shock application.

In addition, the option “Place your order, get it from the store at the time you want” continues. With this new application, Şok Market will provide delivery services to its neighbors in the neighborhood where its stores are located.

Users will be able to shop online from their neighborhood’s Şok Market, and their orders will be sent to their homes on the same day at no additional cost.

Moreover, the prices of the products ordered from Şok in Pocket will be the same as Şok Market prices. In Cepte Şok, all available products of Şok Marketler are offered, from dairy products to delicatessen, from breakfast to edible items, from personal care to home and life categories. In this way, all customers can easily and quickly access the wide and affordable product range they are used to, and products such as Mis, Piyale, Evin, Mintax, which are their own brands of Şok Markets. In addition, users can access the current opportunity products in the stores from the “Opportunities of the Week” and “Campaigns” sections.

Şok Marketler aims to employ a total of 5,000 people in 2020

Uğur Demirel, CEO of Şok Marketler, said in his statement about the application of Cepte Şok: “It is important for us to start this service in this difficult period as a society. We work with our 30.000 employees and business partners to ensure that people reach reliable food. I would also like to thank them. We try to do our part to make our society feel comfortable. For this reason, as Şok Marketler, we started the delivery service to the address.

We deliver the products ordered by our customers online without leaving home, at the same prices as our markets, for free.

We will also provide additional employment for Şokte Cepte. We aim to employ a total of 5,000 people in 2020, both within the scope of the address delivery service and the new stores we plan to open. ”
