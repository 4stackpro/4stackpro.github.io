Goldberg回憶Wrestlemania 20與Brock Lesnar一戰，坦誠觀眾反應讓他「很受傷」(含相關影片) - 格鬥摔角

WWE總裁Vince McMahon在2004年的第20屆Wrestlemania上，傾盡心力，安排一場Goldberg與Brock Lesnar的夢幻對決，甚至安排Stone Cold Steve Austin來擔任特別裁判，增加話題性，不過Vince McMahon沒算到的是，在賽後都將離開WWE的Goldberg與Brock Lesnar，根本就只想敷衍了事，無心應戰，導致比賽內容極其平淡，觀眾的不滿也直接在現場宣洩，該場賽事令人最開心的段落，竟然只有賽後Stone Cold Steve Austin各給雙方一發Stunner，直至現在，這場惡名昭彰的賽事，依然是摔角迷口中近代WWE最糟賽事的代表之一。

※Goldberg與Brock Lesnar在第20屆Wrestlemania上的對決：

日前，Goldberg在接受媒體專訪時，提到了關於這場比賽的結果是如何安排的，Goldberg表示在思考這個問題的時候，你必須把自己當成是Vince McMahon來思考該怎麼樣做決定、討厭誰又少一些，他當時就是站在Vince McMahon的立場來思考賽事結果安排的問題，在當時Vince McMahon知道他跟Brock Lesnar都不會在賽後續留WWE的狀況下，Vince McMahon氣得想宰了他們兩個，不過顯然的Vince McMahon是比較不爽Brock Lesnar，所以最後讓他拿下比賽的勝利，Goldberg這樣打趣的回覆媒體的提問。

不過提到當時觀眾報以滿場的噓聲，Goldberg還是難免表示，當他在擂台上給摔角迷一切的時候，卻得到這樣的回應，其實真的很傷他的心。

Goldberg表示當時觀眾不管怎麼樣就是想噓他們，他當時把Brock Lesnar高舉過他的頭，Brock Lesnar也一樣高舉他並翻摔下來，他認為這應該沒有人能說這不是他們看過最酷的比賽過程，畢竟在過去的比賽當中，沒有任何人對他或是Brock Lesnar這樣做過，所以在這樣的狀況底下，觀眾卻對他們認真比賽的過程不買帳，的確是讓他相當傷心。

但是如果要提到過去沒有人高舉過Goldberg這件事，Goldberg可能忘記了在WCW時期，1998年11月分對上當時擂台名尚為「The Giant」的Big Show，就曾經高高的把他拉起來，給了他一發Choke Slam，雖然不是雙手將人高高扛起的Millitary Press式的拋摔技，但是那個高度也絕對夠高了。

When Brock Lesnar Pulled off a WWE Stunt Inside the Octagon

Daniel Cormier had some of the best moments in the octagon. From beating the best athletes to becoming a two-division champion, he has done it all. Relive one of the most electric moments from his career when he almost brawled with Brock Lesnar inside the octagon.

‘DC’ conquered the light heavyweight championship and moved up a weight class to challenge Stipe Miocic for the heavyweight title. At that time, Stipe had 3 title defenses under his belt, the highest in UFC heavyweight history.

At UFC 226, Cormier stunned the world when he knocked out Miocic in the first round. Hence, cementing him as one of the best combat sports athletes in the world. Post-fight, he delivered an emotional speech where he mentioned his heartbreaking loss to Jon Jones and how he overcame it to accomplish his goals. However, DC wasted no time to call out the biggest name in sports entertainment – Brock Lesnar.

He said, “There’s a guy that I have known for a long time. He’s a wrestler, he’s an All American, he’s a former UFC champion. I never thought I’d fight him. But, Brock Lesnar get your ass in here.”

Brock Lesnar storms to the Octagon, pushes Daniel Cormier

Lesnar—who was in attendance—stormed to the octagon and shoved Cormier. Cormier responded to the situation by saying, “Push me now and get slept later.”

Following the push, ‘The Beast Incarnate‘ took over the microphone and belittled the performances of Francis Ngannou and Miocic. He said:

“Let me tell you somethin’. I have walked into this building and watched heavyweight disasters from the beginning. Ngannou is a piece of sht, Miocic is a piece of sht. DC I’m coming for you motherf****r.” Lesnar slammed the microphone on the camera and ended the interview.

Originally, it was Jon Jones who had called out Lesnar after his win over Cormier. But, due to his suspension, Jones was out of the play. Cormier attempted to seize the opportunity to bank the biggest paycheck of his career.

The potential bout between Cormier and Lesnar had everybody talking. Even rumors about Lesnar entering the USADA drug-testing pool surfaced. There might be some truth to these rumors when you compare his physique before the callout and after the callout, hinting that the WWE star might have stopped using PEDs.

Unfortunately for the fans, the fight never materialized. According to Joe Rogan, Lesnar was unhappy with ESPN’s business model and preferred the old PPV model. Although we could not witness this bout in an MMA cage, there are chances of it happening inside the WWE ring.

On multiple occasions, Cormier has stated his intentions to work for WWE. It’s “never say never” in the pro-wrestling world. Cormier’s teammate Cain Velasquez took a similar route and faced Lesnar last year in the WWE.

WrestleMania 36 results: Brock Lesnar stunned, The Undertaker wins Boneyard Match, Edge outlasts Orton

The WrestleMania 36, WWE’s premier pay-per-view event had plenty of thrills despite being held behind closed doors in the wake of the novel coronavirus pandemic.

Wrestling fans were expected to fill the Raymond James Stadium in Tampa, Florida but WrestleMania 36 was held at closed sets at the WWE Performance Center in Orlando and other, unnamed, locations, with only essential personnel.

In the biggest match of the 2-day event, Brock Lesnar was stunned by Drew McIntyre to win the WWE Championship. In a hard-hitting main event, McIntyre survived a series of F-5s from Lesnar to become the first United Kingdown wrestler to win the world title.

While Undertaker’s win over AJ Styles in the Boneyard Match was one of the highlights of Day 1 of WrestleMania 36, The Edge and Randy Orton delivered a brutal classic on Day 2 in their Last Man Standing match.
