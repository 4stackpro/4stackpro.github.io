Latest worldwide coronavirus statistics from Spain – Breaking news from the USA – Posted after midnight GMT

ACCORDING to the latest Worldometer statistics (at 02.00am GMT today), more than 1,601,302 people have now been diagnosed with the coronavirus (Covid-19) around the world to date, compared to 1,513,935, yesterday.

Of today’s total, 1,150,001 people are currently infected, of which 49,148 (4 per cent) are in critical condition. Out of the remaining 451,301 cases, 355,671 have recovered or have been discharged. However, 95,630 have died from the disease to date.
