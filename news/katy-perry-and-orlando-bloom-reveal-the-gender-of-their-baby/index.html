Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom reveal the gender of their baby

Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom have given fans a new detail about their baby.

The 35-year-old singer revealed on Instagram on Friday that they’re having a baby girl.

Perry posted a photo of a beaming Bloom, 43, covered in what looks to be shaving cream with a pink tint.

Coronavirus UK: Orlando Bloom thanks NHS for ‘saving his life’

Orlando Bloom’s life was ‘saved’ by the NHS during his 20s (Picture: WireImage)

Orlando Bloom has expressed his gratitude to the NHS for once ‘saving his life’ after a horrific accident.

The Pirates of the Caribbean star is one of the growing number of celebrities showing their support for the health service as they bravely continue to fight against the coronavirus pandemic.

So far, the Covid-19 virus has tragically claimed the lives of seven healthcare professionals as of Saturday (4 April). Across the UK, more than 4,300 people have died due to coronavirus while over 41,900 cases have been reported.

Recalling how the NHS has helped him in the past, Orlando told his Instagram followers: ‘The NHS saved my life when I was 20 after I fell from a 3rd floor window and broke my back.

‘Having lived in the US for nearly 20 years now it’s one of the public services from home I’ll always respect the most.’

He accompanied the post with a video of the NHS logo and the statements: ‘Don’t send our NHS backwards. #StayHomeNow.’

Orlando’s tribute touched his followers, including one who is an NHS staff member and commented: ‘NHS worker for 16 years and proud of it.’

Heaping their own praise on the NHS, another nurse weighed in: ‘We are so lucky to have such a great healthcare system in the UK, I work as a nurse & feel so proud of all the medical staff working in this crisis!🌈 sadly it is abused a lot & people take it for granted. I just hope after this storm we can treat it with better respect [sic].’

‘Your heart is so so big!! Thank you for everything Lando, we love you,’ one fan replied, while another said: ‘Im grateful forever for the people that saved your life.’

It’s otherwise a joyful time for Orlando, who is expecting his first child with fiancee Katy Perry.

Following their pregnancy reveal in March, the couple announced this weekend that they are having a girl.

Alongside a snap of the 43-year-old’s face covered in what appears to be pink icing, she simply wrote: ‘It’s a girl’.
