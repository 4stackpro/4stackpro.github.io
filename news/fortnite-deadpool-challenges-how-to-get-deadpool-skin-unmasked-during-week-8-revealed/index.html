Deadpool is walking around this Doncaster village to raise spirits during lockdown

Locals in Edlington have spotted the distinctive anti-hero patrolling the streets during the coronavirus lockdown.

But there’s nothing to fear.

For big hearted Dean Ashbridge has been donning the famed red and black costume of the Marvel Comics favourite to raise spirits among villagers and local children during the COVID-19 shutdown.

Comic and super hero fan Dean, 45, who lives in the village said: “The idea was given to me by somebody who saw me out in my mask.

Fortnite Deadpool challenges: how to get the new skin, and everything else about the crossover event explained

Fortnite Deadpool challenges: how to get the new skin, and everything else about the crossover event explained

Deadpool follows Star Wars and Birds of Prey crossover events

By Alex Nelson Friday, 3rd April 2020, 4:39 pm Updated Friday, 3rd April 2020, 4:47 pm

(Image: Epic Games) Copyright: Other 3rd Party

Popular Battle Royale game Fortnite is currently enjoying a resurgence of sorts as gamers, hemmed in by social distancing and pandemic busting lockdowns, head online to relieve their boredom.

Fortnite’s model of business is such that there is often new content to enjoy, to keep players coming back for more time and again.

In the past, we’ve had crossovers with blockbuster movies like Star Wars and the recent Birds of Prey, and now another R-rated superhero franchise is coming to the game.

Post-modern antihero Deadpool has been making appearances in the game ever since the latest season kicked off on 20 February.

He himself is slated to make an appearance in the game soon, in the form of a brand new skin alongside loot, and a slew of special challenges.

Here’s everything you need to know about it.

What’s the Deadpool crossover event?

Ever since the beginning of Fortnite’s current season, players have been able to access a brand new HQ as part of the game.

And also since the beginning of the season, that HQ has been littered with Deadpool Easter Eggs.

One of the game’s recent challenges had players searching the HQ for ‘Pool’s chimichangas, his fast-food of choice.

In the last few days, players have been able to explore Deadpool’s hideout, which is full of funny details - he’s hoarding toilet paper for instance - and clues as to when the character will be available in the game.

When will the Deadpool skin be available in Fortnite?

One of those clues was a calendar featuring an image of the hero himself (of course).

The calendar was pretty definitive as to when Deadpool’s skin might appear in Fortnite, with 3 April circled in bright red marker pen.

What will the challenges be?

It’s expected that alongside an opportunity to grab the Deadpool skin for yourself and run rampage across the Fortnite map, there’ll be a number of new challenges for players to partake in.

So far, weekly challenges have tasked players with searching the HQ and hideout for items associated with the character, such as his trusty katana swords or… a toilet plunger?

The week 7 challenges go live at 2pm BST, and ask that you find Deadpool’s two pistols.

You can do this by searching in the Battle Pass screen in either Deadpool’s headquarters or the Agents tab; the guns should be found easily.

How do I unlock the Deadpool skin?

To get your hands on the Deadpool skin on 3 April, you’ll need to complete each set of weekly challenges.

Previous weekly challenges related to the skin have been to left click on the computer in Deadpool’s hideout to see the challenges, jump from the Battle Bus without thanking the driver, find his milk carton, look for his chimichangas, find a a toilet plunger, destroy a toilet, look for his katanas, and damage an opponent’s structure.
