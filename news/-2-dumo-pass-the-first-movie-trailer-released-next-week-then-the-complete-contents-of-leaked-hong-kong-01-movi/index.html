【毒魔2】傳電影首預告下周出爐 完整內容隨即外洩？｜香港01｜電影

COVID-19新型冠狀病毒肺炎疫情持續，許多猛片被迫延期上映。然而，由安地索堅斯（Andy Serkis）執導；湯哈迪（Tom Hardy）、米雪威廉絲（Michelle Williams）及活地夏里遜（Woody Harrelson）主演的《毒魔2》（Venom 2），卻無懼疫情威脅，如常上映。早前有幕後製作人員指電影預告將在下周出爐，豈料預告內容近日在網上流出。

由湯哈迪主演的《毒魔2》未有因新型冠狀病毒肺炎疫情影響而延期上映。（《毒魔》劇照）

在首條《毒魔2》的預告中，就有粉絲期待已久的「血蜘蛛」登場。據該名劇透者表示，在Marvel及Sony商標出現後，鏡頭一轉去到三藩市，當時有一把聲說：「新英雄保護這城市。」（A new hero protects the city）。之後大家見到一群小朋友穿上萬聖節服飾，向不同人索取糖果。有一位男士開門，並給他們糖果。而這位男士，就是由活地夏里遜飾演的Cletus Cassady。

新英雄保護這城市，所說的新英雄應該就是毒魔。（《毒魔》劇照）

當時Cletus Cassady戴上面具，並說：「甜味還是爛味。」他然後摘下面具，露出一半人臉及血蜘蛛的臉。他然後笑稱：「我想這是爛味。」接下來就出現警察追捕Cletus Cassady的畫面，後者當時則駕駛著一輛紅色的車，追捕Eddie Brock。之後，一名警探問Eddie Brock哪裡可以找到血蜘蛛。鏡頭之後見到Eddie Brock從報紙上看到有關孤兒院被焚毀的消息，旁白Cletus Cassady：「把奪走別人的命當作純粹藝術。」在下一個鏡頭，終於見到Eddie Brock跟毒魔對話。

活地夏里遜在《毒魔》片尾片段出現，他就是今集反派血蜘蛛。（《毒魔》劇照）

最後，Eddie Brock在空中轉化為毒魔，並爬入血蜘蛛所在的教堂內。然後畫面變黑，旁白Cletus Cassady：「我的目標不只製造死亡。」在預告結束前，有警察向血蜘蛛開槍，但卻被他的共生體觸鬚捉住，並把子彈化成其他東西。之後，警察開始尖叫，而預告也完結。

Eddie Brock再次與共生體聯手對抗敵人。（《毒魔》劇照）

其實當時網上曾流出不只文字版的內容，有網民表示曾看到一條俄羅斯版的《毒魔2》預告。可是，這條俄羅斯版的預告外洩後，立即遭到檢舉。目前再沒有人上載這條預告。據負責視覺效果的幕後製作人員Amit Chaudhari稱：「《毒魔2》預告進入「本地化」流程，並即將在全球推出。」

Remembering the stunning moment The Grateful Dead and Woody Harrelson got stoned in Al Gore’s house

It’s no secret that The Grateful Dead, apart from being the forefathers of psychedelic folk and the cult icons worshipped by ‘Deadheads’, were also massive stoners. Everywhere the band took their mind-expanding live show a trail of roaches could be found.

As well as always exploring their capacity on stage because of their penchant for drugs offstage they became the poster boys of the LSD generation that erupted in California during the ‘60s—for many people they were the overlooked icons of the counter-culture movement.

It’s enough to believe then that the group have certainly had their timeless escapades. One night Jerry Garcia even ate a piece of birthday cake that included 800s doses of acid in the icing (things got a little strange). Even with that in mind, there was one place you wouldn’t have expected them to smoke some weed—in former Vice-President Al Gore’s house, along with Woody Harrelson.

In the early ’90s, before The Grateful Dead disbanded following the death of co-founder Jerry Garcia, the band visited Al and Tipper Gore—a political power couple still in the ascendancy of power. While environmentalist and serving Vice-President between 1993 – 2001, Al Gore was indeed a fan of the band, it was his wife who could be given the moniker of ‘Deadhead’.

The band were welcomed in the Gore’s house and were then joined by Hollywood’s own Woody Harrelson, who himself is a widely known marijuana-smoker, to complete a mix of people unlikely to meet up and have a chat in your local pub—but that is what was happening at the home of the then-current Vice-President of the United States of America.

The political couple then excused themselves to get ready for an event. Now, this may be us adding some colour to the story, but we like to think that Woody then shot a look to the band like one does when your parents leave the room and you’ve got mischief on your mind. But while the pair were in the shower, Woody and Jerry snuck off for a quick joint.

According to former Grateful Dead road manager Cameron Sears, it was a fairly innocuous experience: “Al and Tipper [Gore’s wife, a big Dead fan] were upstairs taking a shower while [the band and entourage] were down there hanging out in their house. Woody Harrelson had come with us, and he and Jerry had gone into the powder room and enjoyed a puff or two.”

He added: “There’s a Secret Service guy standing outside the door. They open the door and it’s like a Cheech and Chong movie!” Though Sears plays down the event, the idea that Woody Harrelson and Jerry Garcia arrive back to the lounge or dining room, stinking of good weed, with red eyes and smiles on their faces while being watched by the Secret Service has us wildly smiling.
