Travis Scott Drops More ‘Fortnite’ Astronomical Tour Merch

Following Travis Scott’s Astronomical Tour on Fornite, which saw the premiere of a new Kid Cudi collab with “The Scotts” as well as a slew of merchandise, the famed rapper has now returned with a variety of new goods to celebrate the final performance of the tour. Unveiling an alternate KAWS-designed cover art for the single, the new drop incorporates new CD, vinyl, and cassette options, as well as a variety of Cactus Jack x Fortnite gaming accessories.

In the drop, fans can find new wearable merchandise from the likes of graphic T-shirts, hooded sweatshirts, and more. The notables, however, are the various gaming accessories, which includes a keyboard wrist pad toting Russian typography, Playstation 4 thumb grips in blue, and Xbox One thumb grips in orange. Other highlights include a blue flame beanie toting the Russian “Cactus Jack” logo, as well as a T-3500 REPEAT long-sleeve T-Shirt in a washed and treated garment-dyed base, with screen printed graphics on the front and back.

Stormi Webster took control of Travis Scott’s Fortnite concert, and the rapper’s reaction was adorable

Stormi Webster interrupted Travis Scott’s virtual Fornite concert called “Astronomical,” the 27-year-old rapper showed on his Instagram story on Thursday.

The “Astroworld” rapper, who shares his daughter with ex Kylie Jenner, held Stormi and asked if she wants to play Fortnite on Instagram Live. “Yeah,” she responded.

“Stormi took over be back one sec,” he wrote on his Instagram story, showing the 2-year-old wearing headphones and manning the controller to play along with the 12.3 million users that participated in the event.

As Scott told Webster to say “bye” and prepared to rejoin the in-game concert, she mumbled, “I don’t want to watch this movie, daddy.”

“Ok. Can’t watch this movie. Be back. Gotta put on a movie. See you later,” he told the Instagram Live viewers while smiling.
