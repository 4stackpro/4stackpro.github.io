BOLLYWOOD heartthrob Sushant Singh Rajput has reportedly taken his own life.

The 34-year-old actor died at his residence in Mumbai.

5 Sushant Singh Rajput was a prominent Bollywood actor Credit: AFP or licensors

Who was Sushant Singh Rajput?

Sushant was a prominent Bollywood actor. His latest role was in ‘Chhichhore’, a box office hit released in 2019.

His debut in Bollywood was in ‘Kai Po Che!’ in 2012, and from there he starred in high-profile movies and TV shows.

The actor was best known for his role in ‘MS Dhoni: The Untold Story’, in which he played the role of India’s revered cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni.

On his death, Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi wrote: “Sushant Singh Rajput…a bright young actor gone too soon. He excelled on TV and in films.

Did Sushant Singh Rajput have a wife?

Sushant was rumoured to be dating Rhea Chakravorty.

The couple were pictured together in Mumbai on several occasions, but denied being in a romantic relationship saying they were just good friends.

In January, Rhea posted on her Instagram page for Sushant’s 34th birthday.

She wrote:“Happy birthday to the most beautiful “supermassive black hole” that is known to mankind! Shine on you crazy diamond @sushantsinghrajput #boywithagoldenheart #rheality”.

Sushant dated Ankita Lokhande for six years, his co-star on TV programme Pavitra Rishta. The couple separated in 2016.

How did Sushant Singh Rajput die?

Sushant Singh Rajput was found dead in his apartment in Mumbai.

He is believed to have taken his own life, according to Mumbai police.

Rajput’s death comes after his former manager Disha Salian, 28, died after falling from a building in Mumbai earlier this week.

সূত্রের খবর, বেশ কিছুদিন ধরে অবসাদে ভুগছিলেন তিনি। চলছিল চিকিৎসাও। তাঁর ঘর থেকে তেমন বেশকিছু কাগজপত্র ও ওষুধ মিলেছে, তা থেকে স্পষ্ট যে,তিনি অবসাদের চিকিৎসা করাচ্ছিলেন। লকডাউনের সময় একাই ছিলেন সুশান্ত। প্রসঙ্গত, কয়েকদিন আগেই আত্মহত্যা করেন তাঁর প্রাক্তন ম্যানেজার দিশা সালিয়াঁ।

সুশান্তের এই আকস্মিক মৃত্যুতে শোকস্তব্ধ বলিউড থেকে শুরু করে ক্রীড়া– সমাজের সব মহল। অভিনেতার মৃত্যুর খবর ছড়িয়ে পড়তেই ট্যুইটারে সকলে প্রতিক্রিয়া জানাতে থাকেন।
