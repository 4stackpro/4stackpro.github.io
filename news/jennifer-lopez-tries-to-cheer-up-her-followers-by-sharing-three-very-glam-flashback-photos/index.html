Jennifer Lopez Flaunts Her Toned Legs In A Hot Pink Mini Dress

JLo sizzled in a tiny fuchsia mini dress covered in feathers.

Jennifer Lopez proudly showed off her long, toned legs in a series of new photos shared to her Instagram account this week. JLo treated her fans to a stunning throwback look at herself during a professional photo shoot prior to the coronavirus lockdown, which showed her in a number of different poses as she embraced her pink side.

The 50-year-old mom of two once again proved she looks years younger than her age in the April 15 upload. She posed in her fuchsia-colored ensemble that had glamorous long sleeves made entirely of thin, fluffy feathers.

The first snap in the three photo upload showed the “Love Don’t Cost A Thing” singer as she crouched down on the white floor in front of a matching all-white background. She paired her bold outfit with matching shiny hot pink heels.

JLo — who recently revealed a bold hair transformation — gazed off into the distance while she posed, proudly showing off her long, tanned legs.

In the second photo, she struck a very similar pose, though that one was taken from the front as she gave the camera a sultry look.

Jennifer stood up to give fans an even better look at her long, tanned legs in the third and final snap. She posed with her right foot in front of her left and wore her long, straight hair flowing down to her waist.

In the caption, the multi-talented star shared a quote from the 2004 Lindsay Lohan and Rachel McAdams movie Mean Girls as she sent her 118.9 million followers some love.

Her fans took to the comments section to give the star a whole lot of love back.

“Yesss queennnnn,” one follower commented, along with several heart emoji.

“You are too beautiful,” added another.

“I swear there isn’t a color that doesn’t look good on you,” a third person wrote, including an emoji with stars for eyes.

The upload has received more than 1.6 million likes in the first 18 hours.

Jennifer didn’t explicitly confirm in the caption when or where the photos were taken, though she did tag some of the team she worked with on the shoot. The tags appeared to confirm the snaps were from a December photo session ahead of her appearance on Saturday Night Live.

JLo’s been keeping her social media fans entertained with plenty of new and old content amid the coronavirus pandemic.

She and fiancé Alex Rodriguez most recently gave fans a peak at how they spent Easter in lockdown with their children. The athlete shared a video that showed the family taking part in an Easter egg hunt in their Miami back garden, which was set up by his wife-to-be.

Sean ‘Diddy’ Combs and Jennifer Lopez reunite for dance party

(CNN) It’s all love between Diddy and J-Lo.

Sean “Diddy” Combs and his ex-girlfriend Jennifer Lopez showed off their friendship and their dance moves Sunday during his Instagram Live fundraiser.

On Saturday the mogul posted on his verified Instagram account “Let’s lift up the energy.”

“Tomorrow we will all dance together for our healthcare workers! Let’s spread some positive vibes and let them know how much we appreciate them!,” Combs posted. “Tomorrow at 3PM EST on my Instagram Live Team Love Dance-A-Thon. THE WHOLE WORLD IS INVITED!!!!!!”

Combs, who recently changed his middle name to “Love,” was promoting Team Love partnering with Direct Relief, “a nonprofit humanitarian aid organization that rushes critical medicines and requested supplies to communities affected by poverty or emergencies throughout the world,” according to its site.
