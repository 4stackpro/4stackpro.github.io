Gwen Stefani gives Blake Shelton a 'quarantine haircut' that looks more faux-hawk than mullet

Three-time Grammy winner Gwen Stefani gave her boyfriend Blake Shelton a trim while quarantining at the 1,382-acre 10 Point Ranch in Tishomingo in his native Oklahoma on Monday.

The 50-year-old pop diva used clippers to cut the sides of his head, which wound up looking more like a faux-hawk than the Tiger King-style mullet she originally attempted back on March 26.

The 43-year-old country crooner then instructed Stefani to carve Tonight Show host Jimmy Fallon’s initials into his head while appearing on the ‘Home Edition’ of his NBC program.
