哥倫比亞礦災 11死4傷

哥倫比亞的庫庫努巴鎮一處煤礦場，4日發生一起爆炸事故，造成11人死亡和4人受傷。(圖取自推特)

哥倫比亞當地消防部門表示，4日在鄰近首都波哥大(Bogota)的庫庫努巴鎮(Cucunuba)一煤礦場，發生一起爆炸事故，造成11人死亡和4人受傷。

該起爆炸造成在礦坑內工作的礦工傷亡。儘管先前哥倫比亞當局為因應俗稱武漢肺炎的2019年冠狀病毒疾病(COVID-19)，而宣布緊急衛生狀態，但礦工仍照常工作。在政府命令下，多數哥倫比亞人正在進行隔離中，但採礦是被豁免管制的活動之一。

昆迪納馬卡省(Cundinamarca)消防局局長法凡(Alvaro Farfan)告訴當地媒體，發生該起事故的煤礦為合法經營，不過他沒有提供有關該起爆炸的更多細節。

而稍早在3日，哥倫比亞東北部聖卡耶塔諾(San Cayetano)的一座煤礦坑也發生類似意外，造成6名礦工遇難。

在此起最新事故發生後，哥國國家礦業局(National Mining Agency)宣布，暫停庫庫努巴的「地下煤礦開採活動」。

Al menos 11 muertos deja explosión de mina en Cucunubá

Colombia coal mine explosion kills 11

Eleven people have died and four more are injured after an explosion in a coal mine in central Colombia, Cundinamarca Governor Nicolas Garcia Bustos says.

“The accident that occurred this afternoon in the municipality of Cucunuba, where there was an explosion inside some mines, leaves us with the tragic toll of 11 people dead and four injured,” Garcia Bustos said in a tweet on Saturday.

Earlier in the day, the governor had said that “between 10 and 12 miners” were inside the mine in the village of Pueblo Viejo in Cucunuba, some 80 kilometres north of Bogota, when the explosion occurred.

Daily newspaper El Espectador had initially cited emergency services as saying some 14 workers were trapped in the mine, before changing the number to 15.

Garcia Bustos said the explosion was believed to have been caused by an “accumulation of gases.”
