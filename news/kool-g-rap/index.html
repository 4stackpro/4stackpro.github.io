“Eminem Reveals His Top Rappers Of All Time”

YouTuber NoLifeShaq has been slipping into the DMs of the biggest names in hip hop to ask the age-old question: “Who is the greatest rapper of all time?” And although Eminem didn’t reply in time to make the video, he did tweet his answer when the episode aired.

First, he named Lil Wayne, Tupac Shakur, Royce da 5’9,” JAY-Z, Redman, Treach, Kool G Rap, The Notorious B.I.G., and Crooked I, “in no particular order.”

For me, in no particular order… Toss up between wayne, pac, royce, jay, redman, treach, g. rap, biggie & king crook…. — Marshall Mathers (@Eminem) June 14, 2020

But he wasn’t finished there. Em then added Redman, LL Cool J, Nas, Joyner Lucas, Kendrick Lamar, J. Cole, André 3000, Rakim, and Big Daddy Kane to his list.

Plus redman, LL, nas, joyner, kendrick, cole, andre, rakim, kane… — Marshall Mathers (@Eminem) June 14, 2020

It’s a more expanded version of the list he offered up in his 2002 single, “Til I Collapse.” Back then he announced, “I got a list, here’s the order of my list that it’s in / It goes Reggie, Jay-Z, 2Pac and Biggie / André from OutKast, Jada, Kurupt, Nas, and then me.”

“Eminem 公开个人评比史上最佳饶舌歌手名单”

YouTuber NoLifeShaq 最近透过个人 Instagram 私讯了将近 30 位知名饶舌歌手，向他们询问在他们心目中各自认为有史以来最伟大的饶舌歌手名单，其中饶舌传奇 Eminem 便在受访艺人之中。不过 Eminem 并未在 Instagram 上回覆，而是透过 Twitter 发文，Eminem 写道：「对我来说，以下的歌手没有特别的排序：「Lil Wayne、2pac、Royce da 5’9”、Jay-Z、Redman、Treach、Kool G.rap, Biggie 和 King Crook。」除了上述这 9 位饶舌歌手之外，第一组名单之后 Eminem 又再加上一则贴文，列入了：LL [cool j]、Nas、Joyner [lucas]、Kendrick [lamar]、[j] cole、Andre [3000]、Rakim、[big daddy] Kane 等人。

事实上这并非他第一次向众人公开他的喜好名单，Eminem 上次他的单曲《Til I Collapse》中的歌词便提及这个主题，歌词写道：「I got a list, here’s the order of my list that it’s in/It goes Reggie, Jay-Z, 2Pac and Biggie/André from OutKast, Jada, Kurupt, Nas and then me.」其中可见绝大多数都与这次相同，相信 Eminem 所言不假，是真的十分喜爱这些饶舌歌手。

“Eminem livre sa liste des meilleurs rappeurs de l’histoire”

Sans ordre particulier.

Récemment, Ludacris avait livré son top 5 des meilleurs rappeurs de l’histoire en prenant surtout en compte le flow et la manière de rapper des artistes pour établir son classement. Aujourd’hui, c’est Eminem qui se livre à cet exercice si particulier et les fans l'écoutent parce qu’en général, il fait partie de la liste. Cette fois, il a juste répondu à un message sur Twitter provenant du compte NoLifeShaq et le Detroiter a livré une liste comprenant Lil Wayne, 2Pac, Royce Da 5’9, Jay-Z, Redman, le rappeur de Naughty By Nature, Treach, Kool G Rap, The Notorious B.I.G. et KXNG Crooked.

Il a fallu environ deux heures pour que Slim Shady réponde à la question, le temps pour lui, certainement, de mettre de l’ordre dans ses idées avant d’envoyer sa liste, “sans ordre particulier” a-t-il précisé. “A vous de choisir.”

“Joyner Lucas fala após Eminem citar seu nome em lista de ‘melhores rappers da história’”

Joyner se mostrou honrado por aparecer na lista.

O rapper Eminem foi até as redes sociais neste final de semana e surpreendeu seus fãs ao interagir com o popular Youtuber ‘No Life Shaq’ que gravou um vídeo perguntando a diversos artistas quem são os melhores rappers de todos os tempos. Ele fez sua pergunta para Eminem no Instagram mas não obteve resposta. Vendo o vídeo enquanto navegava pela Interney, Shady então resolveu revelar sua lista.

“Para mim, em nenhuma ordem específica… Lil Wayne, 2pac, Royce Da 5’9″, JAY-Z, Redman, Treach, Kool G Rap, The Notorious B.I.G, KXNG Crooked, LL Cool J, Nas, Joyner Lucas, Kendrick Lamar, J. Cole, André 3000, Rakim e Big Daddy Kane ” publicou o rapper de “Rap God”. Agora, o rapper Joyner Lucas reagiu após ver que seu nome estava na lista de Slim Shady.

Joyner Lucas foi até as suas redes sociais e compartilhou um print screen das publicações de Eminem e fez uma legenda reagindo. “Quando você jogou basquete e estudou Michael Jordan como o maior, e então Michael Jordan cita seu nome como um dos maiores jogadores de todos os tempos, é assim que isso me faz sentir”. publicou Joyner, indicando que tambem acha que Eminem um dos maiores rappers de todos os tempos.

“Black Thought reveals his top 10 rappers of all time”

Black Thought has revealed his top 10 rappers of all time and his list is full of heavy hitters.

During an Instagram Live interview with Fatman Scoop, The Roots frontrunner listed Rakim, Chuck D, Kool G Rap, KRS-One, Big Daddy Kane, The D.O.C., Rev Run, Schoolly D, EST and LL Cool J as his picks for his top MCs.

Later in the interview, he reflected on his creative process when collaborating with artists like Kool G Rap and Rakim. “If I did a record with G Rap, the nuances of my style that come from G Rap will probably stand out more because that’s who I was paired with,” he said.

“There’s certain artists that are one-fifth of who I feel makes me up and Rakim is one of those dudes,” he continued. “I occupy a dope space where most of the MCs that I have a certain level of respect for, that respect is mutual. That’s all the validity, that’s all really the accolades, that’s all my flowers while I’m still here.”

Aside from music, Black Thought, Questlove and their production company, Two One Five Entertainment, recently signed a first-look deal with Universal Television and Universal Television Alternative Studio. Variety reported that the three-year deal will expand their current relationship with NBC outside of their current role as the house band for “The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon.”

“Los raperos favoritos de Eminem”

MADRID, 15 Jun. (CulturaOcio) -

Eminem ha compartido una lista con sus raperos favoritos de todos los tiempos, y lo ha hecho de una manera de lo más curiosa.

Porque no ha sido en una publicación propia a través de sus redes sociales, sino respondiendo a un tuit de un fan en Twitter.

El youtuber NolifeShaq escribió primero: “Quién es el mejor rapero de todos los tiempos”, acompañando su frase de un vídeo en el que él mismo aparece mandando mensajes privados con la pregunta a raperos como Drake o Travis Scott.

En una respuesta sorpresa al tuit, Eminem desveló su lista de raperos favoritos, que incluye a Tupac, Jay-Z, Kendrick Lamar y more.

“Para mí, sin un orden en particular… me balanceo entre Wayne, Pac, Royce, Jay, Redman, Treach, G. Rap, Biggie y King Crook”, ha escrito.

Y aún ha añdido más nombres en otro mensaje recogido por el NME: “LL, Nas, Joyner, Kendrick, Cole, Andre, Rakim, Kane…".
