【閃電俠】主角童年版演員Logan Williams突離世 終年16歲｜香港01｜即時娛樂

曾於DC超級英雄電視劇《閃電俠》（The Flash），飾演少年版「閃電俠」的加拿大童星Logan Williams於前日（2/4）突然逝世，終年16歲，暫未知道死亡原因。據外國傳媒報道，因為新冠肺炎疫情嚴重，正接受防疫隔離的Logan母親甚至無法獲親友前來致哀，令人感到心酸。而《閃電俠》男主角Grant Gustin亦有在社交網站上載一張與Logan和黑人男星Jesse L. Martin的合照悼念，指相片在2014年拍攝《閃》劇第一集時所拍攝：「我好欣賞佢嘅才華，亦好欣賞佢嘅工作態度。」他又希望網友為Logan及其家人祈禱。另外，1990年版《閃電俠》男主角John Wesley Shipp亦有留言，大讚Logan為角色全情投入。

Logan Williams Dies: ‘The Flash’ And ‘When Calls The Heart’ Actor Was 16

Logan Williams, who played a young Barry Allen on the CW’s hit The Flash and also appeared in Hallmark Channel’s When Calls the Heart, died Thursday at age 16. No cause of death has been revealed.

Logan Williams, far left, in ‘When Calls the Heart’ Hallmark Channel

Canada’s Tri-City News quoted Williams’s mother, Marlyse Williams, saying she is “absolutely devastated” from his death.

The Flash star Grant Gustin, the adult Barry Allen on the CW series, posted a photo of Williams with Jesse L. Martin taken during the 2014 pilot episode.

“I was so impressed by not only Logan’s talent but his professionalism on set,” Gustin wrote. “My thoughts and prayers will be with him and his family during what is I’m sure an unimaginably difficult time for them. Please keep Logan and his family in your thoughts and prayers during what has been a strange and trying time for us all. Sending love to everyone.”

Williams appeared as the young Allen in eight episodes. He most recently was last in the Season 2 episode “The Man Who Saved Central City.” He recurred as Miles Montgomery in multiple episodes of When Calls the Heart and had guest roles on The Whispers and Supernatural (pictured above).

Williams was born in Vancouver and landed his first role at age 10 in the Hallmark film Color of Rain.
