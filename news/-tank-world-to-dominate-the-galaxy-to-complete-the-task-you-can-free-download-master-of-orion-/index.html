《戰車世界》要你稱霸銀河 完成任務可免費下載《Master of Orion》

▲完成《戰車世界》戰鬥任務即可免費下載太空戰略遊戲《Master of Orion》單人模式版本。（圖／戰遊網提供）

記者賴威志／綜合報導

戰遊網（Wargaming.net）正式宣布，旗下遊戲《戰車世界》邀請所有車長們重返銀河宇宙，凡於《戰車世界》完成一場戰鬥任務，即可免費下載廣受好評的經典4X太空策略遊戲《Master of Orion》，獲得稱霸浩瀚銀河的機會。

4 月 9 日至 4 月 23 日期間，玩家只要使用任何階級的戰車於《戰車世界》隨機戰鬥（包括大戰）中完成一場簡單的戰鬥任務，即可透過戰遊網 Game Center下載 《Master of Orion》，免費解鎖單人模式以及所有種族。 車長還將獲得《Master of Orion》獨特自訂要素作為獎勵，為車輛進行客製化！

《戰車世界》全球發行總監Max Chuvalov表示：「 我們很高興讓車長們有機會免費下載《Master of Orion》，透過遊戲征服銀河宇宙，在外太空發揮戰略技能！《Master of Orion》是一款激動人心的策略遊戲，不論是老手或第一次接觸遊戲的新手，都將能感受到遊戲的魅力。立即前往《戰車世界》完成任務並下載《Master of Orion》吧！」

▲這次《Master of Orion》可是包含原版遊戲內容阿！。（圖／戰遊網提供）

在提供玩家免費下載的單人模式版本中，玩家將有機會體驗超過 75 種可研發科技、探索浩瀚銀河中多達100個太陽系、以及透過改造太空飛船等資源爭取最終的勝利。此外，下載內容也包括原版遊戲、「The Revenge of Antares」DLC和來自收藏版中的 Terran Khanate 外星種族與 Retro Fleets。

對於《Master of Orion》的忠實玩家來說，這次開放的版本是由原始開發團隊所研發，保留過去遊戲中獨特星際間的戰爭。在探索《Master of Orion》的過程中您會體驗到：伴隨著精心編排的配樂，一場以遙遠的星系為美麗背景的星際戰爭與探索。 面對充滿敵意的文明、與神秘的外星人談判、和盟友分享知識，一同揭開這個宇宙的新篇章。

World Of Tanks Is Offering Players Master Of Orion Free

Wargaming has a special offer to their fans this week as those who play and beat a mission in World Of Tanks can get a copy of Masters Of Orion. If you’re not familiar with it, Masters Of Orion was one of the all-time classic turn-based sci-fi games to come out on PC back in the day. More recently, they released a new entry in the series back in 2016, developed by Nimble Giant Entertainment and released by Wargaming. Now you can own a copy of the game absolutely free, and all you have to do is beat a mission in WOT. Players just have to complete a simple battle mission in Random Battles (including Grand Battles) between April 9th-23rd using any tier. Once that’s done, you can download Master of Orion with all races and single-player mode unlocked. Which can be downloaded through the Wargaming.net Game Center. You can read more about it below as well as see a trailer for the new version.

“We are excited to give our commanders the chance to take their conquest spaceward, using their tactical skillsets in another atmosphere – for free! For both old players and new, the Master of Orion universe is a boundless and exciting strategy game that will give adventurers the chance to explore the final frontier from their front room. Make sure you jump into World of Tanks right away to download this amazing content,” said Max Chuvalov, Publishing Director of World of Tanks.

“Within the downloadable single-player package, players will have the chance to experiment with over 75 researchable technologies, explore giant galaxies with up to 100 solar systems, modify spaceships to achieve victory – with access to the original game, the Revenge of Antares DLC, The Terran Khanate alien race and Retro Fleets from the collector’s edition. Diehard fans of the initial titles will be relieved to know that this revival was built under the watchful eyes of the original development team – and has retained its unique interstellar warfare and beautiful exploration. Players are invited to experience a fully orchestrated score as they confront hostile civilizations, negotiate with mysterious aliens, share knowledge with allies and uncover this artfully imagined universe.”
