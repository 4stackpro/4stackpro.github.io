UFC 249如期開打 總裁：已找好私人小島

終極格鬥冠軍賽（UFC）總裁Dana White表示，將按照原訂時間在下週舉行的UFC 249，會在某個私人小島閉島舉行，他也強調2019年冠狀病毒疾病（俗稱武漢肺炎）疫情期間最重要的就是健康與安全。

UFC去年12月就宣布UFC 249將於2020年4月18日於布魯克林進行，主秀會是Khabib Nurmagomedov對戰Tony Ferguson。儘管受到病毒疫情影響，日前White宣布Nurmagomedov無法參賽，改由Justin Gaethje對戰Ferguson也不開放觀眾入場，但也表示不會延期，成為全球體壇少數在疫情期間仍舉辦比賽的賽事之一。

而今天White向《TMZ》透露更多細節，表示比賽將會是在私人小島舉行，他們已經找好場地，預計很快就會正式簽約，但拒絕透露這個小島位於什麼區域。此外他也表示未來2個月的期間，他打算每週都在這座格鬥島上舉辦比賽，會邀請許多知名國際選手參加。

包括主秀Ferguson、Gaethje在內，所有選手、團隊成員都會先經過採檢，確定沒有感染病毒才會搭乘飛機前往比賽小島。White表示，安全是首要之務。

White也呼籲格鬥迷不要責怪因疫情無法離開俄羅斯的Nurmagomedov，這也不是格鬥選手自己能決定的情況。同時他也駁斥Joe Rogan無法擔任UFC 249解說的傳聞，表示Rogan不會缺席。

日前曾表示將無懼疫情持續舉行賽事的終極格鬥冠軍賽(UFC)，總裁Dana White今天透過信件通知所有成員，為了因應目前日趨嚴峻的疫情，聯盟已經決定將延後自本周末起的後續三站格鬥之夜賽事（包括3/21, 3/28以及4/11），也將設於拉斯維加斯的辦公室暫時關閉。

「在過去二十個年頭中，我們總是排除萬難地舉行每一場比賽，但這次的狀況截然不同，全世界都遭受疫情影響，現在沒有任何事情比讓你及你的家庭保持健康及安全來得更加重要。」

直到昨日為止，UFC都仍是目前世界級運動賽事中少數選擇續戰的組織。上周末在巴西的賽事是以閉門方式舉行，原定本周末將於倫敦開打的賽事，先因美國政府對歐洲的禁航令而取消，聯盟原先規劃將此戰移至拉斯維加斯的據點舉行，但在內華達州體委會日前頒布暫時中止任何搏擊運動賽事的禁令下，UFC官方最終也不得不放棄本站比賽。

目前UFC官方將復賽目標擺在臺灣時間4月19日在紐約布魯克林舉行的UFC 249，該役主秀將是輕量級的拳王爭霸戰，由生涯未嘗敗績的俄羅斯雄鷹Khabib Nurmagomedov（28勝0敗）對上Tony Ferguson（25勝3敗），Nurmagomedov曾於兩年前的UFC 229擊敗Conor McGregor獲得生涯的第27勝，但賽後與對手陣營爆發激烈衝突，讓Dana White罕見地決定不在當場授予他冠軍腰帶，並給予兩人禁賽處分。McGregor在今年一月份的UFC 246重返賽場，僅花了40秒就擊倒對手Donald Cerrone，而Khabib也預定將於四月的這場賽事復出，同樣備受賽迷關注。

UFC 249 date and start time: Ferguson and Gaethje to fight on ‘private island’ as Dana White reveals fight card

UFC 249 is set to go ahead despite fears over coronavirus and it now has a new headline bout.

It was all set to be a potential classic but the promotion lost their main event fight just weeks before it was set to get under way.

Getty Images - Getty 2 Tony Ferguson and Khabib Nurmagomedov were set to meet at UFC 249, but the champion has to withdraw, with Dana White deciding to replace him with Justin Gaethje

Unbeaten lightweight champion Khabib Nurmagomedov was due to put his crown on the line against former interim title holder Tony Ferguson.

The pair have previously been scheduled to fight on FOUR separate occasions, but each of those bouts has been cancelled for various reasons.

And is seems that curse has struck again for UFC 249, with the champion in his native Russia, which has closed its borders.

Despite that blow, UFC president Dana White has now booked a new main event with Justin Gaethje stepping in to face Ferguson.

2 Dana White teased the possibility of UFC 249 taking place on a private island

UFC 249: Date and UK start time

The UFC main event was originally set for a headline slot in Brooklyn, New York on Saturday, April 18 but that has had to be changed.

While the date has remained the same, a new location and start time has not been confirmed as of yet.

But White revealed he is close to sorting out a venue for the fight, claiming the show is set to go ahead on a ‘private island’.

“I am a day or two away from securing a private island,” White told TMZ.

“I have a private island that I’ve secured; we’re getting the infrastructure put in now, so I’m gonna start doing the international fights, too.

“With international fighters, because I won’t be able to get international fighters into the US, I have a private island that I’m gonna start flying them all in and doing international fights from there.

“So as of April 18th, the UFC is back up and running.

“We have all our own planes and everything. They’re gonna fly into the island, get the fighters.”

UFC 249: Why was the original headline cancelled?

Despite White’s best efforts to finally arrange a Khabib and Ferguson clash, the bout has had to be postponed for the FIFTH time.

Lightweight champ Khabib ruled himself out of his title showdown as he remains in coronavirus lockdown in Russia.

Posting on Instagram, the Russian revealed: “Currently, I am in Dagestan and I am training and preparing every day, although I don’t know what am I preparing for, because after we came to Russia we also learned that the borders are going to be locked.

“Same as the States, same in Europe, Emirates – everywhere. The whole world is in quarantine right now.

“So now I am hearing that they are looking to organise it with or without me. OK, go ahead.

“I am even hearing that they are looking for an opponent for Tony, because he is in the States and I am here in Russia. But I am here not on my own will.”
