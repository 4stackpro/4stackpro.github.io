One Direction四成員重新追蹤贊恩 前老闆低調認「十週年回歸」時間點也曝光

記者蕭采薇／綜合報導

英國夯團「一世代」（One Direction）在2010年成軍，五個帥男孩的魅力曾經席捲全球少女，不過2015年人氣成員贊恩（Zayn）離團，四人體的One Direction多撐了一年，還是在2016年宣布暫時解散，成員們單飛簽約不同公司，如今也各自擁有一片天，不過近來有消息透露，他們將在10週年重組。

▲One Direction被爆將在10週年以「五人體」回歸。（圖／CFP）

One Direction即將在今年七月迎來成軍10週年，雖然成員們已經各奔東西，但卻有種種跡象顯示他們有可能重新聚首。包括奈爾、連恩、哈利和路易4名成員，都已經重新追蹤了贊恩的推特帳號。另外知名歌詞網站「Genius」，也被發現出現一首One Direction新歌《Five Again》，歌名就開門見山表示將會是五人的再一次出發。

One Direction reunion looks to be in the works

(CNN) Can you hear the squeals of delight of the Directioners?

The One Direction fans are in the throes of delight as it looks like their favorite group may actually be getting the band back together.

Now there is hope.

Eagle-eyed followers noticed last weekend that the official One Direction Twitter account and the accounts belonging to members Harry Styles, Liam Payne, Louis Tomlinson and Niall Horan are once again following Malik’s account.

Liam Payne is refusing to fully lift the lid on any One Direction reunion plans – as one of his bandmates has threatened to ‘egg’ him for revealing too much.

The What Makes You Beautiful singers are edging closer to the 10-year anniversary of the moment they were formed on X Factor.

Side note: How has it been that long?!

Amid their hiatus, Liam, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson and Niall Horan have faced intense speculation over a possible reunion – something the dad-of-one has teased himself.

But, discussing those pesky rumours, the 26-year-old told Charlotte Hawkins and Ben Shephard: ‘No, I can’t tell you too much because I’ve already said too much.

‘There’s been a threat from one of the members that he’s going to egg my house if I say any more, so I don’t want that to happen.’

Liam Payne revealed a One Direction bandmate had threatened him over letting slip about reunion plans (Picture: ITV)

To be fair, Liam, we’re currently in lockdown so there will be no egging for a very long time…

Despite the threat of a yolk-covered home, the dad-of one admitted the Night Changes stars have been chatting ‘a lot more’ recently.

‘It’s been nice, we’ve been talking to each other a lot more,’ he continued. ‘The lines of communication are a lot more back open with everyone.

‘I think we’ve realised how special it is to get to a 10-year anniversary of anything really. To have gone as far as we did and be as successful as we were and still be here today and receiving all the things from the fans, it’s really nice.

Liam explained it was ‘nice’ to be back in touch with his One Direction stars (Picture: WireImage)

‘So we’re definitely feeling how special it is and making sure we’re telling each other that.’

We love to see it.

However, Liam basically revealed that Zayn probably won’t be involved in any plans, joking: ‘We’re bringing Alesso in as a fifth member.’

This isn’t the first time the Strip That Down has revealed that a possible One Direction reunion could be in the works.

One Direction are edging closer to their 10-year anniversary (Picture: Rex)

‘We’ve got a 10-year anniversary coming up so we’ve all been speaking together a lot over the last few weeks, which has been really nice,’ he told the Sun.

‘There’s a number of different things that we are all working on to try and make happen and people are forwarding emails around.’

During an Instagram Live session with Alesso, he added that Louis ‘told him off’ for letting slip of their plans.

‘Most of us are in London. We’ve been trying to arrange the first group FaceTime at the moment,’ he confessed.

‘Louis told me off for revealing a bit of our plans the other day. So you’re going to have the group telling me off on the group chat.’

Can someone get us into this group chat, please?!
