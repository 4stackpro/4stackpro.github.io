Dakota Johnson Is As Obsessed With These Skater Shoes As Her Gucci Loafers

Our obsession with Dakota Johnson and her style lies in the fact that her red-carpet persona is an otherworldly Gucci maven, but her everyday aesthetic is decidedly normal. Johnson is just as at ease being photographed on the Met Gala staircase wearing a glittering confection by her friend Alessandro Michele as she is being papped wearing Lycra at Cha Cha Matcha. She has a sense of self-assuredness that pours out of her, and, unlike the Los Angeles pilates set she is embedded in, the actor has a grungy side that she doesn’t hide in Lululemon.

Exhibit A: Johnson’s Vans habit. She has a penchant for the same checkerboard classic slip-ons at the age of 30, as many indie kids and skaters do in their late teens. While she has been known to stride around film sets in Vans Sk8-Hi 38 DX shoes (the high-top trainer style), the black-and-white square printed slippers are her go-tos for running errands.

Dakota wearing Vans in LA in 2019 © AKGS

Johnson shopped for groceries during the Covid-19 lockdown wearing high-waisted micro-stripe jeans, a white vest and Vans – a look that’s indicative of her day-to-day style at home. Her lo-fi formula is one shared by fellow actor Kristen Stewart, who wears her denim low-slung and ripped in comparison to Johnson’s form-fitting cuts, and the Biebers, who wear their Vans with cycling shorts (Hailey) and skater shorts (Justin). In short, she’s not alone in her love for the ’90s footwear made famous by American rockers such as Weezer.

Dakota wearing Vans in LA in 2018 © RB/Bauer-Griffin/Getty Images

During lockdown – when many live in socks and Birkenstocks, felt loafers or, let’s be honest, just slippers – it’s quite frankly impressive that Johnson looks like she could do a kick flip were she actually a closet skater girl. Perhaps that’s the crux of our fascination with Johnson: she can frequently surprise us while still remaining resolutely everywoman.
