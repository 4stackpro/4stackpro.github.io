Office 365 4/21 起易名為 Microsoft 365，新增 AI 應用與範本資源

全球目前已有超過 3,800 萬用戶訂閱 Office 365 服務，微軟 30 日宣布 Office 365 將從 4 月 21 日起成為嶄新的「Microsoft 365」，提供進階的 Office 應用程式、1TB OneDrive 雲端儲存空間，享有專業資安與技術支援，並陸續加入 Microsoft Teams 與 Microsoft Family Safety 兩大體驗。

做為 Office 365 的進階版本，Microsoft 365 是以 Office 為基礎，加入人工智慧、豐富的內容與範本，並由雲端推動的全新體驗。現在 Microsoft 365 新增更多元的雲端應用與範本資源，包括 Microsoft Editor、Presenter Coach、PowerPoint Designer、Money in Excel 等，並加入 Microsoft Teams 與 Microsoft Family Safety。這些功能從今天陸續推出，並將在未來數個月內提供給所有訂閱用戶，以提升在編輯文件、進行簡報等體驗。

Microsoft Editor

「Microsoft Editor」是一項以人工智慧驅動的服務，支援超過 20 種語言，可在 Word 與 Outlook 網頁版使用，並有符合 Google Chrome 與 Microsoft Edge 瀏覽器的擴充功能可安裝。

任何人都能在 Word、Outlook 網頁版以及一般網頁上使用 Microsoft Editor 的基本編輯功能，例如字詞拼寫與基本語法；而 Microsoft 365 個人版與家用版的訂閱用戶，則能使用進階語法檢查與文辭美化等功能，讓用戶在寫作或編輯文件更清晰且精準。

Presenter Coach、PowerPoint Designer

PowerPoint 中的「Presenter Coach」功能同樣是以人工智慧驅動，可以檢測用戶演說的速度是否太快、說出「嗯」的次數是否過多，並協助用戶在排練演講時改善單一語調與說話技巧。這項功能將以免費預覽形式供所有人試用，之後只提供 Microsoft 365 訂閱用戶獨家使用。

相信所有人都希望能在簡報與文件展現創意，以便有效傳達資訊。PowerPoint 中的「PowerPoint Designer」有助於激發創意，例如自動提供數種簡報排版以供套用，或者將文字轉換成美觀易懂的時間軸。

PowerPoint Designer 針對 Microsoft 365 訂閱用戶提供 Getty Images 圖庫上 8,000 多張精美圖片與 175 部循環播放影片，以及 300 種新字體、2,800 個新圖示的獨家使用權限，還有超過 200 款全新範本可套用，協助用戶設計出更具吸引力的簡報檔案。

Money in Excel

Excel 功能特性適用於管理個人或家庭的支出收入，於是微軟進一步推出「Money in Excel」，可以連結用戶的銀行帳戶與信用卡資料，自動導入交易紀錄與帳戶餘額，幫助用戶輕鬆管理、追蹤、分析收入與支出，這項功能將在未來數個月後率先於美國啟用。

Outlook on the web

Outlook 網頁版新增一站式管理功能，用戶可以整合個人行事曆與工作行事曆，行程一目了然，更有效率地管理時間。

此外，微軟在 Outlook Android 版本推出的 Play My Emails 功能結合 Cortana 應用，幫助用戶更從容地掌握最新信件。而 Microsoft Search 更新後可在 Android 與 iOS 平台上識別自然語言，更快速且輕鬆地進行語音搜尋。

Microsoft Teams

Microsoft 365 個人版與家用版的訂閱用戶也能夠使用 Microsoft Teams 了，用戶將能透過建立群組，與家人朋友一起完成生活中的各項計畫，共享日常物品清單，整理共同日曆，存取帳戶或 Wi-Fi 密碼等重要資訊，甚至可查看家人朋友的位置更新，隨時保持聯繫。

Microsoft Family Safety

Microsoft Family Safety 將是市面上唯一能夠同時管理 Windows 電腦、Android 手機以及 Xbox 螢幕使用時間的應用程式，它結合了螢幕使用時間管理、定位追蹤服務、安心駕駛報告以及設備使用限制等功能，在現實世界與虛擬網路全方位守護家庭成員的資訊安全。

微軟將在未來數個月內提供 Microsoft Family Safety 預覽版本，這款可在 Android 與 iOS 平台上使用。

Microsoft 365 個人版與家用版訂閱制服務將自 4 月 21 日於全球同步上市，個人版每個月訂閱價格為台幣 219 元，可同時於 5 部裝置使用；家用版最多可支援 6 位成員、同時於 30 部裝置使用，每個月訂閱價格為台幣 320 元。

微軟再度調整Microsoft 365部分服務品質，以滿足巨量頻寬需求

武漢肺炎在美持續升高，迫使各市要求民眾不要外出，再再讓在家上班的頻寬需求攀高。繼上周調降協同平台Teams的服務品質後，微軟周二再次宣布調整Office 365的部份功能，以維持必要服務的正常運作。

根據一名Reddit用戶周二張貼出微軟於Microsoft 365管理員訊息中心發出的公告，微軟為了在「前所未見」的時期中，維持雲端服務的持續運作，將暫時調整Microsoft 365的特定功能。

上周微軟調降的服務品質包括了檢查線上狀態頻率降低、顯示對方正在打字的時間間隔拉長、以及影像通話解析度等。昨日微軟又再增加包括OneNote、SharePoint和影片頻道Stream三項服務的調整。

首先，企業租戶對Teams中的OneNote 僅提供唯讀權限，除了教育單位外。使用者可以連到Web環境編輯OneNote。OneNote附件檔的下載大小將降到100MB，同步頻率也會降低，不過微軟表示，一般用戶附檔都在50MB以下，因此多數人都不會有影響。

SharePoint部份，微軟會調整區域資料中心夜間和周末時段的特定後端作業排程，像是資料搬移、資料外洩防護（DLP）及新檔案、影像及圖片的上傳延遲，影片回看的解析度也會降低。最後，Stream服務將關閉新上傳影片的人員時間表（people timeline）功能，並將會議錄製的影像解析度調降為720p。

微軟表示依據Microsoft 365的狀況，未來可能還會再做進一步調整。

這段時間Microsoft 365的基礎架構承受極大負荷。微軟企業副總裁Jared Spataro上周表示 Microsoft Teams的每日用戶數從3,400萬增加到4,400萬人，新增1,200萬人，成長率約35%。

Office 365 is becoming Microsoft 365 with new features for the same price

Microsoft is rebranding its Office suite, now dubbing it Microsoft 365 Personal and Family plans, adding some new features to its subscription software. These combine the Office 365 suite with new features and two new apps: Family Safety and Teams for Consumers.

Current Office 365 subscriptions will become Microsoft 365 subscriptions at no additional charge as of April 21 — 365 Personal and Family will keep the price the same at $6.99 a month for one person or $9.99 a month for up to six people. You can also choose the annual route at $69.99 or $99.99 a year.

The full Office 365 suite is here — Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Outlook, Publisher, Access, Skype and Teams — just with some additional features. Plus, you get access on four platforms: Windows, macOS, iOS and Android. You’ll also score 1TB of OneDrive storage per user, so that’s 6TBs on the family plan.

Editor in Word can suggest ways to rephrase a sentence.

Microsoft Editor, which is essentially spell check on steroids, is one of the upgrades. It can alert you if you’re using a word too frequently and even make language suggestions, using machine learning and AI to get a feel for your style. For 2020, Editor will now make style suggestions and even rephrase full sentences. Plus, it will be able to detect unintentional bias language and suggest words or phrases that can be swapped. And for students, there will be a built-in plagiarism detector, ensuring you properly cite sources and preventing you from submitting work that could get you in trouble.

And Editor is moving beyond just Word. It will be included as a browser extension in Outlook, so you can put your best writing forward anywhere on the web, from LinkedIn to Twitter to WordPress.

Here’s an example of the monthly snapshot in Excel.

In Excel land, you’ll get the Money feature, which allows you to keep track of your finances in the classic line-by-line, row-by-row fashion that Excel delivers. You’ll be able to sign in to financial institutions and Excel will import the data. On the left side, you’ll see each transaction presented line by line, and the right will give you an overview of each account. Plus, it will create a monthly spending chart so you can see your habits. It’s a big value-added feature for Excel and eliminates the need for a third-party app. Even better, it could be an easy way to budget money on a personal and family level.

And with the current situation around COVID-19, chances are the lines between work and play have blurred, especially if you’re working remotely. Outlook is introducing a solution in calendars that lets personal and work sit in one cohesive user interface. You’ll be able to import your personal account, like a Gmail, and view it in line with your work calendar. This will be helpful to plan your day and your coworkers won’t be able to schedule meetings on top of important events. Additionally, you’ll be able to import school calendars just from a ZIP code. This way you can be alerted of cancellations, plays, after-hours events and conferences. We’re eager to see these in practice.

Personal and work events can live as one in Outlook.

And these are just a few of the big features arriving with Microsoft 365. It’s great to see that more value is being added at the same price point.

Microsoft will also introduce Family Safety and Teams for Consumers later this year.

Family Safety seems like Microsoft’s version of Apple’s Screen Time. It will work for Windows, Xbox and Android platforms at launch. You’ll be able to see what people in your family are doing on their respective devices and set limits for apps. You can also set content filters by age, effectively blocking some games on Xbox. You can also use this to block specific websites.

Teams for Consumers will arrive later this year.

Teams for Consumers is the company’s Slack competitor that will be arriving for family and friends. And yes, it’s a messaging app with productivity-centric features. Within-group messages, it will sort files, making it easy to find PDFs, Excel sheets, Word docs and even photos. Images and videos will be generated into galleries by default for easy viewing or sharing. You can make a tasks list that anyone in the group can edit, and you’ll be able to assign tasks to a person. It’s also secure with end-to-end encryption. And if you use Teams for work, you can add and use the personal account in the same app.

We’ll know more about these in the coming months.

Microsoft 365 will launch on April 21, but if you can’t wait to get started, you can sign up now and use Office 365 and get many of these features. Here’s the pricing:
