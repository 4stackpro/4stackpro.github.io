艾頓莊孖群星網上開騷 BSB被網民轟不認真

「樂壇大姑媽」艾頓莊（Elton John）主持網上籌款演唱會《The iHeart Living Room Concert for America》，讓樂迷可留在家中抗疫睇騷，同時為食物銀行及幫助兒童的醫護機構籌款。有份演出的包括超新星Billie Eilish、Backstreet Boys（BSB）、瑪麗嘉兒（Mariah Carey）、Alicia Keys、Shawn Mendes、Camila Cabello、Sam Smith等。艾頓莊打頭陣演出，他起初指自己正在隔離的家中沒有鋼琴而無法演出，其後成功借到電子琴，並唱出《Don’t Let The Sun Go Down On Me》，他表示︰「注意安全，珍惜你愛的人，當疫情過去及結束，我會再為大家演出。」

天后瑪麗嘉兒就演唱1996年的名曲《Always Be My Baby》，唱畢她指在家中都戴手套，並在家中與子女慶祝50歲生日。Billie Eilish就與音樂監製哥哥Finneas O’ Connell坐在梳化唱出成名曲《Bad Guy》。男子組合BSB的五位成員分別在各自的家中演唱大熱作品《I Want It That Way》，但被網友批評他們態度不認真，所唱歌曲又跟防疫無關，成員AJ McLean在一堆波鞋前演唱，似在晒波鞋，隊友Nick Carter一度不願離開椅子，而他與成員Kevin Richardson更讓兒子入鏡，Kevin兩子更幫手彈結他和打鼓。

Backstreet Boys reunite for iHeartRadio’s Living Room Concert for America

(CNN) The world may be social distancing amid the novel coronavirus pandemic, but nothing was going to stop one of the most successful boy bands of all time coming together in a good cause.

The group – AJ McLean, Howie Dorough, Nick Carter, Brian Littrell and Kevin Richardson – drenched fans in nostalgia by belting out their 1999 hit “I Want It That Way” from their respective homes on five different screens.

As they were all live in different states, they streamed in their pre-recorded performance, which featured cameos from some of their children.

While Carter held his son Odin as he sang by the pool, Richardson’s sons Mason and Maxwell showed off their musical prowess on the guitar and drums.

BACKSTREET Boys thrilled fans last night when they performed their hit single I Want It That Way with their kids at home while isolating from the coronavirus.

The five-piece joined a wealth of stars including Mariah Carey, Billie Eilish, Alicia Keys, Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello for iHeartRadio’s Living Room Concert for America.

The charity concert, which aired on Fox, saw the group sing their 1999 single, which went to number one in 25 countries and reached number six on the Billboard 100.

Broadcast from their living rooms, Kevin Richardson, 48, lead the group as he held maracas while his sons Mason and Maxwell played the guitar and the drums.

Fellow member Nick Carter, 40, followed suit and danced with his cute son Odin, three, next to his outdoor pool.

Meanwhile, Brian Littrell, 45, AJ McLean, 42, and Howie Dorough, 46, sang from different rooms in their respective houses.

Brian kicked started the fun-filled performance by paying tribute to the “frontline responders that are putting themselves in harm’s way to protect their fellow citizens.”

Earlier this month, the 90s boyband were forced to postpone their upcoming tour to promoting their 10th studio album DNA.

So instead fans were treated to the group reforming virtually during the benefit concert, showcasing their iconic dance moves and harmonies from home.

iHeartRadio’s Living Room Concert for America was hosted by Elton John, which replaced the postponed iHeartRadio Music Awards on Fox.
