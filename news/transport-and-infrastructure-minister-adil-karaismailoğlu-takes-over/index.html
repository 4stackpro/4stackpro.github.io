Transport and Infrastructure Minister Adil Karaismailoğlu Takes Over

Minister of Transport and Infrastructure Adil Karaismailoğlu, in his speech at the handover ceremony held at the Ministry, reminded that as of the end of September, he started to work as Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Transport and Infrastructure with the favor of President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan and Minister Cahit Turhan. Stating that he is always proud of working at the Ministry, Karaismailoğlu said, “We tried to do important works by making use of our valuable minister’s valuable knowledge and experience. We learned a lot from him in such a short time. ”

Karaismailoğlu continued his words by stating that he would endeavor to be worthy of the trust of the President who had assigned this task to him:

“We will do everything we can to be worthy of this task. There are very important projects ahead of us. There are vision projects that will bring voice to the whole world and contribute to the development and future of our country. We will run them all together quickly. While doing these, we will definitely benefit from the knowledge and experience of our respected Minister. I thank him very much for his contributions to our Ministry and Government. On behalf of all my friends, I always thank you again.

Turhan: “I Transfer The Entrust To Our New Minister”

Minister Turhan, who handed over the duty, stated that they did many services related to the access and transportation of the country while he was in office. Duties that should be sacrificed for our nation for our country. They assigned this task to us as the Ministry of Transport and Infrastructure in the first cabinet of our President in the new state system. It was an important responsibility and an important task. We have always been aware of this. Like our Ministers who served in the previous periods, I transfer the trust I received to our new Minister, Adil Karaismailoğlu, who will carry out this task from now on. May Allah help him and his helper. ” Turhan said that they have carried out their duties within the Ministry with their colleagues within the 20-month period he has served, and that they have carried out important projects under the leadership of President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan. Turhan stated that the service caravan will continue without stopping.

“We have taken our precautions early in the Coronavirus Point”

In the current outbreak of coronavirus point in the world Turhan said that Turkey’s less affected by the general, taken without delay of its most important reason was underlined that the implementation of the measures. Turhan said, “Of course, our ministry is one of the ministries in this country where projects and services that the nation expects from the state come to the fore. In this process, it carried out and organized its activities both in domestic and international transportation, especially in line with the decisions taken by the Ministry of Health and the Science Board. Hamdolsun today, with the implementation of these decisions in our country, we did not experience any major problems in social and economic life. After that, we, as the Ministry, should try to get rid of this disaster by paying the least price by applying the decisions taken by our Government together. While leaving this position, I felt the need to share these humble expressions by taking refuge in the tolerance of our Minister. Under the leadership of our President, I believe that the Ministry of Transport and Infrastructure will do better services with you and our new Minister under his captain. I wish you success in your future work. ”

Adil Karaismailoğlu kimdir?

Trabzon’da 1969’da doğan Karaismailoğlu, Trabzon Lisesi’ni bitirdikten sonra Karadeniz Teknik Üniversitesi Mühendislik Fakültesi Makine Mühendisliğinden mezun oldu.

Yüksek lisansını, Bahçeşehir Üniversitesi Kentsel Sistemler ve Ulaştırma Yönetimi programında tamamlayan Karaismailoğlu, Mayıs 1995’te İstanbul Büyükşehir Belediyesi (İBB) Ulaşım Koordinasyon Müdürlüğünde ilk görevine başladı.

Karaismailoğlu, İETT Genel Müdürlüğünde mühendis ve yönetici olarak görev yaptı. 2002 yılından itibaren İBB Trafik Müdürlüğünde trafik kontrol merkezi, sinyalizasyon, akıllı trafik sistemlerinden sorumlu müdür yardımcısı olarak çalışan Karaismailoğlu, 16 Kasım 2009’da Ulaşım Koordinasyon Müdürlüğüne atandı.

Ardından Ulaşım Daire Başkanlığı görevine getirilen Karaismailoğlu, ulaşımla ilgili, İstanbul genelinde birçok projenin hayata geçirilmesine katkı sağladı.

Karaismailoğlu, Temmuz 2016’da Başbakanlık Toplu Konut İdaresinde (TOKİ) İstanbul Emlak Dairesi Başkanlığı görevine atandı. 26 Nisan 2018’de İstanbul Büyükşehir Belediye Başkanlığı Genel Sekreter Yardımcılığı görevine getirilen Karaismailoğlu, Eylül 2019’dan beri Ulaştırma ve Altyapı Bakan Yardımcılığı görevini yürütüyordu.

Karaismailoğlu, evli ve iki çocuk babası.
