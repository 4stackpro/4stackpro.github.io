Rupert Grint and Georgia Groome's relationship as they expect child

Rupert and Georgia have been dating since 2011 (Picture: Getty)

Rupert Grint and his girlfriend Georgia Groome are expecting their first child together and it was a lot to take in.

The world of Harry Potter and Angus, Thongs and Perfect Snogging becoming one was something some of us never expected (or even knew about).

Despite the fact the two have been dating for a while, many were still shocked at the news.

What? How? Who? And most importantly, when?

Expecting their first child

The couple are expecting their first child (Picture: LRNYC / MEGA)

The Harry Potter actor’s rep confirmed their exciting baby news in a statement shared with Metro.co.uk.

’Rupert Grint and Georgia Groome are excited to announce they are expecting a baby and would please ask for privacy at this time,’ it read.

And the pregnant actress was then later seen in north London with Rupert, showing off her baby bump.

Boy, do we feel old.

When did they start dating?

Basically a decade, you say? (Picture: Twitter)

The two have been notoriously private about their relationship and fans didn’t even find out they have been dating for a while until long into their relationship.

In December 2018, the world went into a frenzy discovering Rupert and Georgia. have been dating since 2011 after seeing a photo of them on Twitter.

It’s still not confirmed exactly how long the adorable couple have been dating, but almost a decade has passed.

The photo circulated the internet with the caption: ‘I was today years old when I found out Georgia from Angus, Thongs and Perfect Snogging has been going out with Ron Weasley since 2011?’

Alongside the tweet was a snap of them together, with Georgia’s arm around Rupert.

What have they said about their relationship?

They have known each other since they were young (Picture: McCormack/Knotek/REX/Shutterstock)

Both stars hadn’t said much about their relationship in interviews until Rupert sat down and spoke with The Guardian shortly after turning 30.

He opened up about taking the next step in life and his plans about having a child.

Chatting to the Guardian, he said: ‘Turning 30 felt strange. It just doesn’t feel like I’m there yet and I don’t know what the future holds.

‘I’m just going to go with the flow, keep playing interesting characters and see what happens.’

‘I’d like to settle down and have kids soon.’

Are they married?

The two even sparked wedding rumours (Picture: Alo Ceballos/GC Images)

Rupert and Georgia were spotted walking around Highgate, London, in April 2019 wearing what looked like wedding bands.

The actress sparked rumours that she and the Harry Potter actor had tied the knot in secret after wearing a gold band on her ring finger.

She was enjoying lunch with Rupert and two friends and the ring could be seen glinting away on her left hand.

However, Rupert’s similar band was worn on his right hand, and a rep for the actor denied they had got married.

With how good the duo are at keeping things low-key, we guess we’ll never know.

Rupert Grint, Georgia Groome expecting their first child

Rupert Grint and his longtime girlfriend Georgia Groome are expecting their first child. File Photo by John Angelillo/UPI | License Photo

April 11 (UPI) – Rupert Grint’s publicist has confirmed the Harry Potter actor and his actress girlfriend Georgia Groome are expecting their first child.

“Rupert Grint and Georgia Groome are excited to announce they are expecting a baby and would please ask for privacy at this time,” a representative for the couple said in a statement Friday.

The child’s sex and anticipated birth date have not been disclosed.

Grint, 31, recently starred in the Apple TV+ series Servant and Groome, 28, is known for her roles in London to Brighton and A Fish Out of Water.

They have been dating since 2011.

Harry Potter star Rupert Grint is expecting his first child with actress girlfriend Georgia Groome.

The statement reads: “Rupert Grint and Georgia Groome are excited to announce they are expecting a baby and would please ask for privacy at this time.”

The 31-year-old became a household name after being cast as the role of Ron Weasley, wizarding sidekick to Harry Potter in the hugely successful movie franchise which spanned eight films, Grint enjoying a starring role in each.

Georgia Groome attends the UK premiere of ‘Double Date’ at The Soho Hotel on October 10, 2017 in London, England. (Photo by Karwai Tang/WireImage) More

He played the character between 2001 and 2011, starting as an 11-year-old with next to no acting experience.

Groome, herself a child actress, had starring roles in 2006 crime drama London To Brighton and 2008 teenage dating comedy Angus, Thongs And Perfect Snogging.

The pair have been an item since 2011, though their relationship has largely been kept under wraps.

Grint has discussed fatherhood in the past. He told The Guardian: “Turning 30 felt strange. It just doesn’t feel like I’m there yet and I don’t know what the future holds.

“I’m just going to go with the flow, keep playing interesting characters and see what happens.

“I’d like to settle down and have kids soon. If I had a son, would I call him Ron?

“It’s quite a good name, but probably not. And Grint’s a tough name to pair a one-syllable first name with.”

While former co-stars Daniel Radcliffe and Emma Watson have enjoyed Hollywood success since final movie Harry Potter and Deathly Hallows - Part 2, Grint has mainly focussed on television.

His roles include Sky One comedy Sick Note, crime drama Snatch and Agatha Christie adaption The ABC Murders.

His last movie role was 2015 French film Moonwalkers. He also voiced Josh in Postman Pat: The Movie in 2014.
