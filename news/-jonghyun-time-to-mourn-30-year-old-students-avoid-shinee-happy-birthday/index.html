Friends And Fans Of SHINee’s Jonghyun Share Love On His Birthday

Friends and fans of SHINee’s Jonghyun have shown their love for him on his birthday.

Jonghyun was born on April 8, 1990 and #HappyJonghyunDay trended at No. 2 worldwide shortly after midnight on that day this year as fans shared love and remembered moments with him on the special day.

SHINee’s official Instagram shared a photo of him on April 8 and included the hashtags SHINee, Jonghyun, and his birthday.

SHINee’s Key posted a photo of Jonghyun from a time they went out to eat together. He wrote in the caption, “Happy birthday. I miss you so much. I always feel sorry, and I always love you.”

Girls’ Generation’s Taeyeon posted an image from the 2008 movie “Ponyo,” including a cake emoji in the caption. She tagged herself as Ponyo and Jonghyun as Sōsuke.

Fans noticed that Red Velvet’s Yeri commented on one of Jonghyun’s Instagram posts to say, “Happy birthday, I love you so much” with a heart.
