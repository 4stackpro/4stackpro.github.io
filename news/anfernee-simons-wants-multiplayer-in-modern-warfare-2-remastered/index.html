Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 Remastered rumored for Monday release

Just a few days after files for Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 Remastered were discovered, it’s now rumored that the campaign will drop on Monday, March 30.

Following the discovery of files pointing towards a remaster of Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2, there’s now talk that the campaign could be released as early as Monday, March 30. While we’re still awaiting official announcement from Activision, speculation over the weekend pointed towards a release today.

Prominent Call of Duty leaker Okami was pretty confident when asked about the previously mentioned date of March 30, giving it a “99.9999%” chance (via MP1st). He even pinned the original tweet from March 25 as a double-down that March 30 will be the release of Modern Warfare 2 Remastered.

Files for ads, digital banners and updated cover art for Modern Warfare 2 Remastered were discovered in the latest update for Call of Duty: Modern Warfare. The files suggested that only the campaign would be remastered, although dataminers also uncovered files pointing towards new multiplayer maps from MW2 as well.

Nothing official has been announced by Activision yet but that’s not too surprising. There were tons of leaks ahead of the Call of Duty: Warzone‘s launch and Activision kept tight-lipped the entire time. With little to no actual marketing ahead of time — beyond the rumors and word of mount — Activision announced Warzone and dropped it the very next day.

It’s possible they could be taking a similar approach with Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 Remastered. It’s important to note that this remaster will be only of the campaign and not the entire multiplayer experience, although maps from the game are said to be coming to Modern Warfare.

To be perfectly clear, this is only a rumor and nothing more than that. However, Okami does have a pretty good track record with both the Call of Duty: Warzone leak and the claim that the MW2 remaster won’t be multiplayer (confirmed by datamining).

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 Remaster Reportedly Launches Tomorrow

Reports claim that a remastered version of Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 will launch tomorrow, March 31.

Eurogamer reports that a PlayStation Store listing for the game, which has since been removed, is accurate and that Modern Warfare 2 Campaign Remastered will launch March 31.

The rumours of a remastered MW2 campaign have been long standing and come from several sources. Initially, at least in recent history, it began on Reddit, where a datamine into Modern Warfare Season 3 found details of the then unreleased Call of Duty Warzone, as well as details on a MW2 remaster.

Subsequently, Modern Warfare 2 Campaign Remastered was rated by a South Korean games rating board, and a Twitter account called Okami - which previously accurately dated Call of Duty Warzone ahead of launch - suggested Modern Warfare was releasing March 30.

While there is no sign of the game so far today, Eurogamer reports that it will be the following day. That report suggests that the remaster has actually been finished for a long period of time, with Activision awaiting the right time to release. If reports are true, then it would appear that Activision considers this time as good as any.

The Modern Warfare 2 remaster allegedly only remasters the campaign of the original game. Multiplayer maps from the 2009 favorite are reportedly set to be added to Modern Warfare (2019).
