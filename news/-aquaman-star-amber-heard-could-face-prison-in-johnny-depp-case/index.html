‘Aquaman’ Star Amber Heard Could Face Prison For Fake Evidence Against Johnny Depp

A new report reveals that Aquaman star Amber Heard could face prison time if she is found gulty of manipulating evidence against her ex-husband Johnny Depp.

The intense drama surrounding the legal battle between Fantastic Beasts star Johnny Depp and Aquaman star Amber Heard started when the latter claimed she was being physically and verbally abused during their short-lived marriage. Now, Depp is suing Heard for defamation over the abuse claims.

International Business Times reports that Heard could face up to three years in prison if she is found guilty for falsifying evidence against Depp. Heard claimed that she suffered two black eyes the night before appearing on The Late Late Show with James Corden in 2015. However in a court document, Heard’s stylist Samantha McMillen stated that the actress didn’t appear to have any bruises on her during that time:

“Throughout the day of December 16, 2015, I could see clearly that Amber Heard did not have any visible marks, bruises, cuts, or injuries to her face or any other part of her bod.”

Aquaman Character Information

Aquaman or Arthur Curry is a superhero and one of the founding members of the Justice League. He is a half-breed with both human and Atlantean traits. This is due to his mixed parentage.

His father Thomas Curry is a lighthouse keeper while his mother is the Atlanna is the queen of Atlantis.

Atlantis is a hidden city under the ocean. It has many tribes ruled by a monarch. The residents of Atlantis are called Atlanteans. They are a technologically advanced race.

Atlanteans are built for life underwater. Only the royal members of Atlantis can breathe air. Arthur is the best of both worlds with human and Atlantean features.
