Could Shia LaBeouf be quarantining with a familiar face?

The 33-year-old “Honey Boy” star was photographed exercising and cozying up to ex Mia Goth in Pasadena, Calif., on Thursday, and it appears both of them are wearing what appear to be wedding rings.

In photos, LaBeouf, or should we say La-Buff, shows off his tattooed chest during a run with his lady friend, followed by a romantic bike ride.

The actor, who was previously married to Goth, was in high spirits as he was filmed dancing near a parking lot, before the pair went on a bike ride together. The couple even cruised by a wedding dress store.

Along with his ring, LaBeouf was wearing a pair of Nike running tights and a Megadeth t-shirt, while Goth was sporting navy leggings and a plain white tank top with her giant diamond ring.

More photos and video show the couple taking a rest on a bench for a cigarette and a little PDA. In one image, Goth plants a kiss on his cheek as the actor smokes.

It’s not the first time the former Disney star was photographed wearing the ring. He first sparked marriage rumors when he was spotted wearing the band at the Oscars in February.

LaBeouf and Goth, 26, were married in Las Vegas in 2016. The pair filed for divorce in September 2018, and the actor began dating Robert Pattinson’s ex-fiancée, FKA Twigs, who co-starred with LaBeouf in his recent film “Honey Boy.”

“The separation is amicable, and all details pertaining to the divorce proceedings will remain private,” a rep told Page Six at the time of his split from Goth.

A rep for LaBeouf did not immediately return our request for comment.

Shia LaBeouf buys a Pasadena new build for $5.475 million

Actor Shia LaBeouf has purchased a newly built home in Pasadena for $5.475 million, according to sources not authorized to comment publicly on the deal.

Designed to evoke the period revival styles of the early 20th century, the Mediterranean-inspired residence sits on a third of an acre near the Huntington Library. A fountain and terrace sit outside the roughly 4,100-square-foot house, which has arched windows and wrought ironwork. An ornate frontispiece sits above the entry.
