The Chase: Who is the new chaser? Who is Darragh Ennis?

The Chase is a popular game show on ITV and it sees host Bradley Walsh walk a panel of contestants through a series of questions in a bid to win cash. The aim of the game is for the contestants to not get caught by the chaser, a professional quizzer who tries to stop them from winning the prize. A new chaser has just stepped up to try and stop the contestants in their tracks, and here is everything you need to know about Darragh Ennis.

Who is new chaser Darragh Ennis?

Fans may have seen the new chaser, Darragh Ennis, before as he was a former contestant on the game show back in 2017.

Following his performance, he was asked to join the rest of the chasers, and he will be the first new recruit to join since 2015.

Ennis tweeted about his new role, saying: “I’m really excited to be the 6th chaser on #TheChase. I’ve been working hard on this for a while now and can’t wait for my episodes to air. Well, the ones I win anyway.”

Former contestant Mark Wallace welcomed him to the team. He said: “Congratulations! As a former contestant, always been hugely impressed by the Chasers.

“Plus @anne_hegerty was incredibly nice to us all even while trying to destroy us.”
