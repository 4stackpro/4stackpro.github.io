MAFS: Michael knew Stacey and Mikey were messaging on Snapchat

Married At First Sight’s Michael Goonan and Stacey Hampton finally called it quits during Sunday night’s season finale.

The 29-year-old company director was unable to get over his suspicions that his ‘wife’ had slept with another groom, Mikey Pembroke, earlier in the season.

And speaking to 9Now on Monday, Michael revealed that he had already suspected something was going on between Stacey and Mikey weeks before their alleged one-night stand was exposed at the reunion dinner party.

‘Sneaky form of messaging’: Married At First Sight’s Michael Goonan (pictured) already suspected something was going on between his ‘wife’, Stacey Hampton, and Mikey Pembroke weeks before their alleged one-night stand was exposed at the reunion dinner party

Michael claimed he had discovered they were messaging each other on Snapchat the night before the infamous ‘Hayley incident’.

‘The Hayley incident’ (referring to the night he got drunk and kissed another bride, Hayley Vernon, behind Stacey’s back) took place during an 11-day production break in either late September or early October. The reunion dinner party was filmed on January 15.

‘I seen the night before [kissing Hayley], Stacey was getting messages on Snapchat from Mikey. And I was like, “What the hell’s going on?"’ he said.

‘“Oh, we’re just friends, we’re just friends”. Well, don’t write on Snapchat. Sneaky form of messaging, do you know what I mean?’

‘What the hell’s going on?’ Michael apparently discovered Mikey (left) and Stacey (right) were messaging each other on Snapchat the night before the infamous ‘Hayley incident’

Later that night, fellow groom Ivan Sarakula had a beer with Michael and told him Stacey and Mikey were messaging and had arranged to meet for coffee.

‘I blew up, I was like “Mikey’s a dog!” Like, what the hell?’ Michael said.

According to Michael, Mikey then came to his room and the pair agreed to discuss the matter off camera. Mikey apparently said that he and Stacey were ‘just mates’.

‘He [Mikey] goes, “She asked me for coffee”. And I knew that he was telling the truth. And I was like “Dude, I really like her. Back off,"’ Michael added.

Michael went on to explain that he partied with Hayley in Chris Nicholls and Vanessa Romito’s room later that night, a decision he now ‘regrets’.

‘I was there. Hayley was in the room and stuff. I lost a level of respect for Stacey with what Mikey had told me. I still shouldn’t have been in that room, I still shouldn’t have done that. It was a juvenile way to handle it,’ he said.

Michael’s alleged kiss with Hayley happened that night and became the focus of much of the season until the reunion dinner party when Stacey and Mikey’s rumoured affair took centre stage.

He added: ‘[Stacey] made me feel so guilty, really crippled me. Yet, [she] had this massive secret… this whole time. Stacey denied it to the end, denied it to the end. But honestly, I know it’s true. I know for a fact it’s true.’

Michael is now dating another MAFS bride, KC Osborne, and the couple are currently self-isolating together in Melbourne amid the coronavirus pandemic.

“It didn’t add up.” YouTube

While he says he desperately wanted to believe the woman he fell in love with on the dating experiment, proof they were messaging was the final nail in the coffin.

“Then she says there’s no messages, and out comes the phone,” he said, still baffled she’s still lying about sleeping with Mikey. “I still wanted to defend her and I think the world of her. She’s a great mum, lovely person and sat on a big secret, which would have been really hard to handle.”

What bothered Michael most, however, was the fact she made him feel guilty for a drunken kiss when she was hiding a more sinister secret.

“She made me grovel and jump through hoops and feel so guilty, she really crippled me. It was one of the hardest things I’ve ever had to face,” he continued. “Especially in front of everybody. It was gut-wrenching.”

They are no longer on speaking terms. Nine

So what happened after the dinner party wrapped and the cameras stopped rolling? Michael told Nova 100’s Chrissie, Sam & Browny that he tried to chat with Stacey about these damaging allegations, however, she’d dug a hole too deep to claw her way out of.

“She continued to tell me it never happened. I said there was too much doubt for me to continue in this catastrophe so let’s just have a hug and I’ll see you later,” he said. “But she said she didn’t want to hug me… At the end of the day, I should be the one sort of stamping my feet and saying that I’m the victim.”

Although Stacey maintains Mikey created this scandal in an attempt to grab headlines, he recently passed a polygraph test, scoring far above the standard number for truthful answers.

After four hours of rigorous questioning, the polygraph expert said: “It is my opinion that you are in fact being truthful to that issue. There was no strong emotional responses indicative of deception to the declaration.” The controversial groom obtained an average of 10 on each answer— a truthful answer should be three or more meaning he more than tripled the required criteria.

We’re set to see the former couple go head to head on Sunday night. Nine

In spite of, Stacey has been questioning the legitimacy of his polygraph results and spoke to a trained polygraph operator who suggested his test was inaccurate because he wasn’t asked directly whether they had an affair.

As we know, before undergoing the examination, Mikey wrote in a declaration: “I, Michael Pembroke do hereby declare that while at the Sky Suites apartments I had sexual intercourse with Stacey Hampton.”

“If we were doing it we would ask the person directly, ‘Did you have sexual intercourse with such and such?’ We wouldn’t get the examinee to sign a statement and then test him on what was written on the statement,” the polygraph expert told Stacey in a video she recorded and shared to her Instagram story. “That’s one step removed and it’s not addressing the issues, it’s about the statement.”

“His polygraph test is not credible.” Instagram

Moments later, Stacey shared a message on her Instagram story that read: “The company I called explained that they saw footage of Mikey and said that is an incorrect way to do a polygraph test.”

She fired up reality star continued: “You do not ask someone to write a statement then refer the questions to the statement. Rather, you simply ask direct questions. Anyone can say anything in statements. His polygraph test is not credible. Case closed. Don’t ever mention any “M” word or name to me again. Unless it’s money. LOL.”
