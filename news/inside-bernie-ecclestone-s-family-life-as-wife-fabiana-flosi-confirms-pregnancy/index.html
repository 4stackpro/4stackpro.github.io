Inside Bernie Ecclestone’s family life as wife Fabiana Flosi confirms pregnancy

Bernie Ecclestone revealed wife Fabiana is pregnant with his fourth child (Picture: Getty)

Bernie Ecclestone is set to become a dad for the fourth time, as wife Fabiana Flosi is pregnant with their first child together.

The former Formula 1 boss, 89, confirmed the 44-year-old will be giving birth to a baby boy in July.

‘Yes, it is due in the summer,’ he told Swiss newspaper Blick on Thursday. ‘Hopefully he’ll learn to play backgammon soon!’

‘Like all parents we have only one wish,’ the mum-to-be continued. ‘The child must be born healthy.

‘Hopefully he will never express any interest in doing something in Formula 1!’

Bernie and Fabiana, who is 45 years his junior, married in 2012 at his ski chalet in Switzerland, three years after they first started dating.

Fabiana is set to welcome a baby boy with Bernie in July (Picture: Getty)

The little one will be the racing supremo’s fourth child – he shares daughter Deborah, 65, with first wife Ivy Bamford, and welcomed 35-year-old Tamara and Petra, 31, with second wife Slavica Radic.

Despite her dad being among the richest people in the world, Deborah has chosen to shun the spotlight, keeping her life firmly private.

In contrast, Tamara embarked on a career in the limelight, fronting various sports competitions and shows from a young age.

As well as appearing in two reality shows, she also presented the Red Bull Air Race for Channel 4 in 2006, and followed in her dad’s footsteps by appearing on Sky Sports Italy’s F1 coverage.

Not too shabby.

Bernie is dad to daughters Deborah, Tamara (L) and Petra (R) (Picture: Getty)

Tamara is ‘thrilled’ for her dad and Fabiana (Picture: Getty)

Tamara is currently married to businessman Jay Rutland, with the pair tying the knot in a lavish ceremony back in 2013.

They share a £45million home in Kensington, where they live with their adorable daughter, Sophia.

Bernie’s youngest daughter, Petra, has also lived much of her life in the public eye.

She made headlines back in August, 2011, after marrying James Stunt – whom she shares children Lavinia, James and Andrew with – in a stunning wedding in Italy, forking out around £12million for their big day.

However it wasn’t to last, and they finalised their divorce in 2017.

Petra Ecclestone (L) finalised her divorce from James Stunt in 2017 (Picture: Getty)

Petra spawned her own menswear line, FORM, back in 2008, but the brand went bust just 14 months later.

And, despite having an insanely successful career, Bernie has always held a close bond to his children.

In fact, Tamara revealed she slept in her parents’ bed until she was nine-years-old.

Speaking on an episode of her reality show, Tamara’s World, back in 2017, she confessed: ‘I slept in my parents’ bed until I was nine.

‘I slept on my dad’s side and my sister on my mum’s. Even when we travelled we’d all share our bed.’

Sounds… cosy?

Bernie and Fabiana have a 45-year age gap (Picture: Getty)

Tamara was also the first member of the family to speak publicly on her dad’s happy news.

A spokesperson confirmed to Metro.co.uk she has known for some months, telling us: ‘Tamara is absolutely thrilled for them both’.

A rep for Petra added that the sisters have said: ‘We are delighted by the news and very happy for them.’

The dad-to-be has faced criticism over Fabiana’s pregnancy, as well as their 45-year age gap, but he’s having absolutely none of it.

Blasting the backlash, he told the Daily Mail: ‘There’s nothing that unusual is there? I haven’t had a job for a little while so I have had plenty of time to practise!’

Good to know…

‘I’m very happy and happy really for my wife, she’s been looking forward to this happening for quite a few years so I’m happy she’ll have someone after I’m gone,’ he added.

Naquele ano, Fabiana era casada com um médico e Bernie tinha um matrimônio com a modelo Slavica Radic. Em 2010, o dirigente se separou da croata em um dos divórcios mais ‘caros’ da Inglaterra, tendo que desembolsar o equivalente a 1,7 bilhão de reais em valores da época.

Dois anos depois, a brasileira e o britânico se casaram em uma cerimônia secreta em Gstaad, na Suíça. Após o matrimônio com Ecclestone, Fabiana foi presença constante nos GPs da F1 até a saída do dirigente do comando do esporte, no começo de 2017.

A brasileira também fez parte da Comissão de Mulheres no Esporte a Motor da Federação Internacional de Automobilismo (FIA), que busca auxiliar pilotos do sexo feminino em diversas categorias pelo mundo.

Em 2016, a mãe de Fabiana, Aparecida Schunck, foi sequestrada em São Paulo. Foram cobrados R$ 120 mi para liberá-la, mas o pagamento não foi feito e ela foi resgatada pela polícia. Na época, Bernie disse que “não pagaria um centavo” pela sogra.

Desde a saída de Ecclestone do comando da F1, que agora tem a norte-americana Liberty Media como dona, o casal tem uma residência fixada em propriedade rural na cidade de Amparo, no interior do estado de São Paulo.

Fabiana Flosi, chi è la moglie di Ecclestone/ Avvocato di origini italiane di 45 anni

Fabiana Flosi diventerà mamma a 45 anni e renderà padre Bernie Ecclestone per la quarta volta a 89 anni. Tra la signora Eccleston e l’ex boss della Formula 1 ci sono ben 44 anni di differenza, ma nonostante tutto, i due, dopo essersi incontrati casualmente, non solo si sono sposati, ma hanno anche deciso di allargare la famiglia con l’arrivo di un figlio. Ma chi è Fabiana Flosi il cui nome sta facendo il giro del mondo? Come riporta la Gazzetta dello Sport nell’articolo che annuncia la quarta paternità di Bernie Ecclestone che entra di diritto nella classifica degli uomini diventati genitori in età avanzata, Fabiana Flosi viene presentata come un avvocato di origini italiane diventata ufficialmente la signora Ecclestone nel 2012.

FABIANA FLOSI, L’INCONTRO CON BERNIE ECCLESTONE NEL 2009

Fabiana Flosi e Bernie Ecclestone si sono incontrati per la prima volta nel 2009 durante il Gran Premio del Brasile nel 2009. Fabiana è entrata nella vita di Eccleston tre anni dopo aver divorziato dalla seconda moglie Slavica, con la quale era stato sposato per 24 anni. Fabiana ed Ecclestone si sono sposati il 26 agosto 2012 con una cerimonia sontuosa che si è tenuta a Gstaad in Svizzera, in forma privata, a cui hanno partecipato pochissimi invitati. Come segno del suo amore, in occasione del matrimonio, Ecclestone ha donato alla sua terza moglie un anello del valore di 100mila sterline. Tra pochi mesi, quell’amore che li ha uniti, sarà coronato dall’arrivo di un figlio.
