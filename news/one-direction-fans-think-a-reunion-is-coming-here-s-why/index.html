One Direction fans think a reunion is coming. Here’s why

Is a One Direction reunion coming? Fans of the band sure think so, according to People magazine.

“One Direction” fans are speculating that a reunion with all five original members of the band may be in the works after noticing that Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, Niall Horan, and Liam Payne had begun following Zayn Malik (who left the group in 2015) and the official One Direction account on Twitter, according to People.

Just last week, Payne told The Sun that there are plans in the works for the band’s 10-year anniversary in July, though he said he couldn’t reveal many details yet, according to Teen Vogue.

One Direction went on an “indefinite hiatus” in 2015. Since then, there have been rumors a possible reunion, according to Teen Vogue.

“I think it’ll happen at some point, we’d be stupid not to,” Tomlinson said in January, according to the New York Post.

Fans have also noticed that One Direction’s former website has been reactivated and all of the group’s albums are back on Apple Music, according to the Post.

One Direction fans have been in a “frenzy” on Twitter discussing the band possibly reuniting, according to People.

Liam Payne says a One Direction reunion is on the cards - but will Zayn Malik be there?

It’s nearly 10 years since One Direction were launched into the world via Simon Cowell’s talent juggernaut, The X Factor.

Now Liam Payne says the 1D boys are in talks to mark their decade milestone in July.

Image: Payne says ‘it’s a real good time for us to connect together again’

However, while Payne told The Sun he’d been in contact with Harry Styles, Niall Horan and Louis Tomlinson, he made no mention of Zayn Malik.

“We’ve got a 10-year anniversary coming up so we’ve all been speaking together a lot over the last few weeks which has been really nice.

Image: Malik has not been complimentary about the band since leaving

“To hear a lot of people’s voices and seeing old content and different things that we haven’t seen for a long time or never seen before, it’s very interesting.

“At the moment I’m not sure what I’m allowed to say. There’s a number of different things that we are all working on to try and make happen and people are forwarding emails around.

“But more than anything it’s just been a real good time for us to connect together again.”

Malik quit One Direction in 2015, leaving the band as a four-piece.

The boys went on indefinite hiatus the following year.

Following his departure, Malik has called the band’s music “generic” and claimed he never really wanted to be part of it.

In various interviews since going solo, the band members have all variously implied they were never close.

So far, the youngest member of the band, Harry Styles, has had the most success in his solo career, releasing a critically acclaimed album, touring the world and co-chairing the 2019 Met Gala.

Malik has released two solo albums, adopting a more alternative R&B style.
