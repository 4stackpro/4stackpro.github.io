無界瀑布螢幕 vivo發表NEX 3S旗艦5G手機- SOGI手機王

vivo 今日（3/10）發表新一代 5G 旗艦手機 NEX 3S，規格大致延續 NEX 3 的配置，保留 6.89 吋無界瀑布螢幕、星環影像系統，搭載 Qualcomm Snapdragon 865、LPDDR5 RAM 與 UFS 3.1 儲存容量，具備 5G SA / NSA 雙模，支援最高 44W 超級閃充，提供電話秘書、Hi-Res 音效認證。此外，vivo 也同步推出 NEX 極簡瀏覽器功能。

vivo NEX 3S 共有 8GB + 256GB、12GB + 256GB 規格版本，建議售價分別為 4,998、5,298 人民幣（約 21,461、22,750 台幣），配色方面提供「琥珀醇」、「深空流光」、「液態天河」等款式。vivo NEX 3S 即日起在中國開放預購，其中「深空流光」與「液態天河」將於 3/14 上午 10 點在中國上市；「琥珀醇」則預計 3/21 正式開賣。

vivo NEX 3S 延續前一代螢幕配置，採用 6.89 吋 18.8：9 比例 FHD+ Super AMOLED 無界瀑布螢幕，螢幕左右兩側保持 90 度曲面設計，螢幕占比高達 99.6%，具備曲面螢幕氣氛燈、DC 調光技術。搭載 Qualcomm Snapdragon 865，支援 Wi-Fi 6、藍牙 5.1，並未提供 microSD 記憶卡擴充。續航方面，内建 4,500mAh 電池，支援 44W 超級閃充。vivo NEX 3S 維持 NEX 3 的星環影像系統，後置 6,400 萬畫素主鏡頭（F1.8 光圈）+ 1,300 萬畫素 120 度超廣角鏡頭（2.5cm 微距、F2.2 光圈）+ 1,300 萬畫素 52mm 長焦鏡頭（20 倍數位變焦、F2.46 光圈），支援 RAW 檔拍攝、人眼追焦、Hrper-HDR 功能。前鏡頭採用升降式結構設計，結構內設有 1,600 萬畫素鏡頭、LED 補光燈與聽筒，提供人像美顏、AR 萌拍模式。▲vivo NEX 3S 採用基於 Android 10 設計的 Funtouch OS 10 作業介面。▲Funtouch OS 10 内建物理動效引擎，可提高螢幕操作的流暢度。▲vivo NEX 3S 採用 E3 Super AMOLED 螢幕，支援 DCI-P3 100% 廣色域、HDR 10+ 認證。▲vivo NEX 3S 內建零感均熱板，可協助手機表面溫度降低 4 度，處理器降低 10 度。▲vivo NEX 3S 搭載 X 軸線性馬達，除了提供 4D 遊戲震感效果外，也具備仿真機械鍵盤效果，能夠還原青軸、紅軸與茶軸的回饋觸感。▲vivo NEX 3S 支援 SLA 智慧網路加速、HPUE 技術，可搭配 Wi-Fi 6 進一步提升聯網體驗。▲vivo NEX 3S 支援全功能 NFC，可在中國享有 eID、模擬門禁卡，或是搭乘 42 個中國城市的大眾運輸工具。▲vivo NEX 3S 支援超級影片防手震功能，透過在超廣角鏡頭設置 EIS 電子防手震，可相比普通鏡頭還原 40% 的視覺補償範圍。▲vivo NEX 3S 極簡瀏覽器主打在極簡的操作介面下，保留最大程度的應用功能。

Unity Pharmacy at NEX shopping mall to temporarily close after employee contracts COVID-19

SINGAPORE: The Unity Pharmacy outlet at NEX shopping mall in Serangoon will be temporarily closed for deep cleaning after a part-time staff member was diagnosed with COVID-19.

All employees who were in close contact with the part-time staff member have also been put on a leave of absence, the pharmacy said in a media advisory on Wednesday (Apr 1).

Unity said the affected staff member, who worked as a retail assistant, was last at work on Mar 30. The employee then visited the doctor on Mar 31 for a fever and has since been on medical leave.

On Wednesday evening, the employee was diagnosed with COVID-19 and has since been hospitalised.

Unity said it is extending all the necessary assistance to the affected staff members and the employee’s family during this time.

The affected Unity outlet will be closed for two days for deep cleaning procedures in line with the National Environment Agency’s guidelines and will re-open on Apr 3.

“Unity is monitoring the situation closely and is working with authorities to assist them with contact tracing and ensure all stores are safe for customers and staff,” it added.

Earlier on Wednesday, NTUC FairPrice said its outlet at Bedok Mall will be closed for three days after an employee tested positive for COVID-19.

The employee has been quarantined and is being monitored by medical personnel. The employee, who was diagnosed with COVID-19 on Tuesday evening, last went to work on Mar 25.
