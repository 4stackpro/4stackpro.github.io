川普、3M敲協議！進口1.67億副N95口罩 主要來自中國廠

民視

台灣大學公共衛生學院院長詹長權今（8）日在臉書上發文報喜！詹長權說明，美國公共衛生學院暨學程學會（ASPPH）在官網上宣布台大公衛學院榮獲「2020年美國ASPPH哈里森．C．史賓瑟（ Harrison C. Spencer）傑出社區服務獎」，這個獎項是ASPPH從2018年開始，每年頒發給長期在公共衛生領域上對社區永續經營有卓越貢獻的公衛學院或學程，前兩年都是美國的學校獲獎，如今台灣成了唯一一個美國以外的獲獎國家。詹長權表示，台大公衛學院從1999年開始，就與連江縣政府和彰化縣政府合作，長期進行以社區為基礎的整合性公共衛生服務，提供社區居民健康篩檢、地方公衛專業人員培養等。因為成效亮眼，建立學界與社區資源共享與合作的模式，而受到ASPPH肯定，成為唯一一個美國以外國家的獲獎者。詹長權受訪時提到，因為山地以及離島的醫療資源不平等，便與兩地政府合作。公衛服務主要著重民眾慢性病與癌症的預防醫學，詹長權也說，他們會透過篩檢的結果得知地區與癌症的關係，他說：「像馬祖地區的胃癌就蠻嚴重的。」因此台大公衛學院希望推動早期篩檢，師生專程坐船去馬祖，在當地衛生單位協助下，到各社區幫居民抽血篩檢，除了胃癌外，也整合子宮頸癌、口腔癌、慢性病等篩檢。詹長權指出，透過早期篩檢，就可以找出生病的人，並轉介到醫院治療，經過多年努力，讓馬祖的胃癌發生率明顯下降，後來彰化縣也加入合作，推動萬人癌症健檢等活動。至於社區公共衛生服務成果，詹長權提到，由於台大公衛學院提供的這個計畫，讓這些醫療資源不足的地區民眾平均壽命因此延長、身體也更加健康。他最後表示，原先這個獎要在ASPPH的年度大會上頒獎，但受到新型冠狀病毒引發的肺炎（COVID-19，俗稱武漢肺炎）的關係改成視訊會議，雖然如此，但ASPPH主席Dr. Laura Magaña親自來函通知得獎訊息，也已決定將在明年的年度大會上正式表揚。ASPPH也會在官方網頁上介紹公衛學院的這段影片，讓全世界都能看到台灣在公共衛生領域上的優秀成果。（民視新聞網／綜合報導）

Why 3M Stock Jumped Tuesday

What happened

Shares of The 3M Company (NYSE:MMM) popped on Tuesday, rising as much as 7.1%. As of 12:55 p.m. EDT, the stock was up 5%.

The maker of everyday products from bandages to hearing solutions, saw its stock climb as the Trump administration ended a spat with the company over 3M’s mask production and sales.

So what

On Monday evening, the White House said it planned to buy about 167 million masks from 3M over the next three months in an effort to provide gear to medical workers.

Leading up to this order, President Donald Trump had criticized the company’s efforts to supply masks to Americans during this pandemic. But after talking to 3M CEO Mike Roman, Trump says he was happy with the company’s efforts.

“3M and the Administration worked together to ensure that this plan does not create further humanitarian implications for countries currently fighting the COVID-19 outbreak, and committed to further collaborate to fight price gouging and counterfeiting,” wrote 3M in a blog post Monday about the arrangement.

Now what

3M is importing these masks from its manufacturing facility in China. This means the imports will supplement its monthly U.S. production capacity of 35 million N95 respirators.

“As a global company, 3M has manufacturing operations around the world to serve local and regional markets,” the company said. “As the pandemic unfolds in different stages around the world, 3M will continue to work with governments to direct respirators and other supplies to serve areas most in need.”

Trump Clinches Respirator Mask Deal With 3M

The Trump administration and 3M (MMM), the U.S. manufacturer announced a joint initiative to import 166.5 million of its N95 respirator masks over a three-month period.

Under the terms of the plan, starting in April, 3M will import 55 million masks a month into the U.S. for three months, mainly from its flagship plant in China. The plan will allow 3M to continue to distribute U.S. produced respirators to Canada and Latin America, where 3M is the primary supplier.

In addition, 3M said that it agreed with the Trump administration that it would be cracking down on the recent price-gouging and counterfeiting of its respirator masks.

“We share the same goals of providing much-needed respirators to Americans across our country and combating criminals who seek to take advantage of the current crisis”, said 3M chairman and CEO Mike Roman. “These imports will supplement the 35 million N95 respirators we currently produce per month in the United States.”

The U.S. administration confirmed that it was committed to address and remove export and regulatory restrictions to enable this plan.

Since the coronavirus outbreak in January, 3M had ramped up the production of N95 respirators and doubled its global output to 1.1 billion per year, including the 35 million a month in the U.S. 3M said that it had taken actions to double its capacity to 2 billion globally within 12 months, with additional capacity to begin coming online in the next 60 to 90 days. In the U.S., 3M expects to produce 50 million N95 respirators per month in June, a 40% increase from current levels.

Wall Street analysts take a cautious stance on 3M. The company’s Hold analyst consensus rating is based on 4 Holds, 2 Sells, and 2 Buys. The average price target of $156.40 suggests potential room for a 6% gain in the next 12 months. (See 3M’s stock analysis on TipRanks)
