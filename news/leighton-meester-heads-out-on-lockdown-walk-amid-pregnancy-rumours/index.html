Leighton Meester heads out on lockdown walk amid pregnancy rumours

Leighton Meester steps out with husband Adam Brody and daughter Arlo (Picture: Backgrid)

Leighton Meester was seen heading out with Adam Brody and their daughter Arlo for their daily walk during coronavirus lockdown, amid rumours she is pregnant again.

The Gossip Girl star, 33, nailed the casual look we’ve all become accustomed to in quarantine, as she stepped out wearing a grey t-shirt and navy linen dungarees.

She pulled her hair back into a bun and wore sunglasses as she took a stroll with Adam, best known for playing Seth Cohen in The O.C.

Stopping for a stretch here and there, Leighton looked pretty relaxed while Adam was on daddy duties and pushed their little girl Arlo, four, in her pram.

He was seen sporting a blue checked shirt and pale pink chinos, while covering up with a baseball cap and shades.

Trying to go incognito, are we?

The couple’s outing was no doubt a much-welcomed break from lockdown, as measures restricting movement have been put in place across the US, much like the rest of the globe, to try and control the spread of coronavirus.

Adam and Leighton’s outing comes amid swirling rumours that she is pregnant and expecting baby number two.

Metro.co.uk has reached out to reps for Leighton and Adam.

They welcomed daughter Arlo back in 2015 and speaking to GQ, Adam previously said that becoming a dad has given him a ‘new perspective’ in life.

He said: ‘It’s the best in every way. It just gives you a new perspective in the best way—something to focus on that’s greater and more important than yourself.’

The couple are known for being notoriously private when it comes to their personal life.

Touching on why, Leighton told Refinery 29: ‘I don’t talk about Arlo very much. I am very proud of that area of my life, but I’m also really proud of the show and the work I do.

Acostumbrados últimamente a recibir malas noticias cada día, siempre es un rayo de esperanza transmitir un instante de felicidad como el que viven los actores Leighton Meester y Adam Brody, que esperan la llegada de su segundo hijo.

La pareja, recordada por sus papeles de Blair Waldorf y Seth Cohen en ‘Gossip Girl’ y ‘The O.C.', respectivamente, siempre se ha caracterizado por mantener su vida privada alejada del foco mediático. Pero la intérprete no ha podido evitar que unas fotos suyas luciendo un avanzado estado de gestación hayan sido captadas por los paparazzi en plena cuarentena.

La llegada de una nuevo integrante a la familia Meester-Brody coincide con su sexto aniversario de boda. Tras conocerse en 2011 trabajando juntos en la película de dibujos animados ‘El viaje de Arlo’, los intérpretes se casaron en una ceremonia secreta en California en 2014 y dos años después daban la bienvenida a su primera hija, Arlo Day Brody.

La actriz, rodando ‘Gossip Girl’. (Getty)

Sobre la ‘obsesión’ de los fans con el romance real que viven sus televisivos personajes, Brody confesaba en una entrevista que nunca han visto las respectivas series que les lanzaron a la fama. “Una vez al año tenemos un día de Blair y de Seth, nos disfrazamos. Nos encanta reírnos de ello. A nosotros no nos emociona hacerlo, pero nos gusta que a los fans sí”, aseguraba el actor.
