ASTRO’s Cha Eun Woo In Talks To Star As Lead In Drama Based On Hit Webtoon “True Beauty”

ASTRO’s Cha Eun Woo may be starring in a new drama!

On April 23, news outlet Ilgan Sports reported that Cha Eun Woo has been cast as the male lead of a drama based on the popular webtoon “True Beauty.”

In response to the reports, Cha Eun Woo’s agency Fantagio commented, “Cha Eun Woo has received an offer to star in the drama ‘True Beauty,’ and he is currently reviewing the offer.”

“True Beauty” is a story of a young woman who doesn’t want to be caught without makeup on, and she experiences a heart-fluttering love story with a man who is the only one who knows what her bare face looks like. Even before the drama was confirmed, virtual casting was a hot topic due to the webtoon’s popularity, and Cha Eun Woo was the top pick among netizens.

Cha Eun Woo was offered the role of the handsome Lee Su Ho. He is a perfect man who is the top student at school. Although he steals the spotlight wherever he goes, he is not interested in anyone and is not amused by girls lining up to confess to him. Contrary to his stoic character, Lee Su Ho turns red when he’s embarrassed, and when he’s flustered, he blurts out whatever is on his mind and sometimes goes off the deep end. However, he is actually a kind-hearted guy who always regrets shooting off his hot temper.

Bon Factory will be in charge of production, and writer Lee Si Eun of the “Rude Miss Young Ae” series will write the script. Casting is currently underway with the aim of broadcasting in the second half of this year.

If Cha Eun Woo decides to accept the role, this will be his third drama as the male lead after “My ID is Gangnam Beauty” and “Rookie Historian Goo Hae Ryung.”

These Idols Best Fit The Female Lead with Cha Eun Woo in Webtoon-Based Drama

ASTRO’s Cha Eun Woo is currently in talks to be the male lead of the upcoming drama based on the famous webtoon series, “True Beauty,” and netizens are now excited as to who will complete the list of casts.

Cha Eun Woo’s label, Fantagio, confirmed something about the aforementioned news, saying, “Cha Eun Woo has received an offer to star in the drama ‘True Beauty’ and as of the moment, he is taking a look at it.”

The webtoon series that is set to be given life, which tells the story of a young lady who is into makeup and falls in love with a man who knows her real beauty.

Prior to the news, this had been a topic online over and over on social platforms. A lot of K-pop idols have been suggested to cast the said project, and Cha Eun Woo is one of the best picks among the mob.

Netizens are now asking about who will be the female lead role. As expected, fans made several suggestions on who will be the best fit as the ASTRO member’s leading lady.

Jisoo (BLACKPINK)

Many have said that the woman in “True Beauty” totally resembles Jisoo, from her face, hair, and all.

“Since ASTRO’s Eunwoo was reportedly to be the male lead for ‘True Beauty,’ we’re sincerely hoping for #JISOO to be cast as Jugyeong, even if it seems difficult for YG. Please let this be Jisoo’s big acting break!” Tweeted by a fan. Unfortunately, YG recently confirmed that the BLACKPINK idol has retired from her acting career.

Bona (Cosmic Girls)

Others say Bona will fit the role. Aside from being a lookalike of the said webtoon character, she already showcased her acting prowess in the previous drama Hit the Top.

“Petition for Bona and Eunwoo to be cast for the true beauty drama please and thank you,” according to a fan.

Joy (Red Velvet)

“Joy and Eunwoo for the adaption of true beauty prayer circle,” said one fan. The female star already embarked on the acting scene, such as her role as Yoon So-rim in The Liar and His Lover and as Eun Tae-hee in Tempted. Some feel that her personality and visuals suit the lead role.

Although the said the forthcoming series is still in talks, netizens can’t help but be overwhelmed with the idea that the well-acclaimed webtoon will finally be adapted with their dream character.
