This TikTok shows a smarter way to make dalgona coffee while minimizing dishes

Dalgona coffee is a whipped coffee drink named after a South Korean candy that has become popular online while people are social distancing.

Making the drink is an intense process without a hand mixer and is said to take more than 400 stirs using a whisk.

This TikTok from @melliemelanie showcases a different method of whipping the coffee that takes less effort and fewer dishes.

Dalgona coffee, the whipped coffee drink that takes its name from a South Korean toffee candy, has been taking over TikTok and Instagram as the dominant quarantine food craze. While the ingredients — equal parts instant coffee, sugar, and hot water — for the drink are relatively simple, it can take a bit of effort to whip them into the drink’s signature froth if you don’t have a hand mixer. Whisking can take so much effort that dalgona coffee is also known as “coffee stirred 400 times.”

This TikTok, from @melliemelanie, proposes an alternative method that not only allows for easier mixing but also minimizes dishes. Combine all of the ingredients into the glass you plan to drink out of rather than a bowl, and then insert a whisk and rub it between your palms so it whips the mixture. Once it’s thickened enough, pour milk over the top and add ice.

While the method doesn’t provide the quite the same kind of two-toned look that has been dominating Instagram feeds over the past few weeks, it certainly yields results.

Praktis! Resep Dalgona Coffee Tanpa Mixer, Blender, atau Whisk

Resep dalgona coffee masih banyak dicari oleh netizen. Mereka yang tidak punya mixer, blender, atau pengocok kawat (whisk), bisa memanfaatkan resep praktis ini.

Dalgona coffee menjanjikan tampilan Instagramable sekaligus rasa yang nikmat. Minuman kopi susu ini diracik dari bahan sederhana yaitu susu segar, bubuk kopi instan, dan gula pasir. Dalgona coffee bisa disajikan panas atau dingin sesuai selera.

Untuk membuat busa kopi dalgona coffee yang kental dan creamy bisa menggunakan beberapa teknik dalam mengocoknya. Cara paling praktis tentu saja menggunakan mesin otomatis seperti mixer, blender, atau hand blender. Cara ini dijamin lebih cepat dan tidak bikin kamu lelah.
