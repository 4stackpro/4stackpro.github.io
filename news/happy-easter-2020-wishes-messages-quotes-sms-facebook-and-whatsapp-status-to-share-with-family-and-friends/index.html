New Delhi | Jagran Lifestyle Desk: Easter or Pascha is an annual Christian festival that will be observed on April 12 to commemorate the rise (resurrection) of Christ from the dead. It is observed on the third day after his crucifixion by Romans at Calvary, as per New Testament. On this day, he washed away all our sins and came to be known as the Sun of God.

Easter marks the culmination of Passion of Jesus– a 40-day period of fasting, penance, and prayer. it is time to come closer to God, a time to rejoice and remember the victory of Jesus over death.
