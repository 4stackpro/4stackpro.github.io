【e＋車路事】2020 年度風雲車出爐 Kia Telluride 爭氣奪冠

一直以來汽車界都是由歐日品牌瓜分天下，而近年韓國車開始漸露頭角，在各大年度汽車獎項中勇奪殊榮，而剛公佈的 2020 World Car of the Year，便是由 Kia Telluride 獲得。

World Car Award 每年均會選出各界別年度風雲車，今年便是由來自 25 個國家，共 86 位專業評判組成小組進行評分，考慮的項目包括車廂空間、性能、性價比、安全、創新年眾多項目。最終 Kia Telluride 以 758 分的輕微優勢，力壓第 2 位獲得 745 分的 Mazda 3，以及 738 分的 Mazda CX-30，獲得 Car of the Year 大獎。

Mazda 3 獲 World Design of the the Year 設計大獎。

此外，今次 Porche Taycan 亦同時獲得 World Performanace Car 及 World Luxury Car 獎項，而 Kia Soul EV 電動 SUV 則獲得 World Urban Car 獎，而 World Design of the the Year 設計大獎，則由 去年大改款的 Mazda 3 獲得。

Kia Telluride seven-seat SUV wins World Car of the Year 2020

The Kia Telluride has been voted 2020 World Car of the Year ahead of two Mazda finalists and more than 50 contenders globally.

The Kia Telluride seven-seat SUV has won the 2020 World Car of the Year award, displacing the other two finalists, the Mazda3 small car and Mazda CX-30 small SUV.

The Kia Telluride was voted the winner – from an initial list of more than 50 contenders – by 86 jurors from 24 countries around the world.

It is the first time Kia or any South Korean car company has won the award in its 16-year history (since 2005).

The vehicle is currently sold in North America (USA and Canada) and the Middle East, but CarAdvice understands the Kia Telluride and its twin under the skin, the Hyundai Palisade, are due in Australian showrooms some time in the next 12 months.
